Youdas/(Yəhūdāh)

1  Youdas/(Yəhūdāh), slave of_Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one/messiah, and brother of_Yakōbos/(Ya'aqov), to_the ones called having_been_loved by god the_father, and having_been_kept in_Yēsous chosen_one: 2 mercy to_you_all, and peace, and might_have_multiplied love.
   3 Beloved, making all earnestness to_be_writing to_you_all concerning the common salvation of_us, I_had necessity to_write to_you_all, exhorting you to_be_contending for_the faith having_been_given_over once to_the holy ones.
   4 For some people sneaked_in long_ago having_been_previously_written for this judgment, ungodly ones, replacing the grace of_the god of_us into wantonness, and disowning the only master and master of_us, Yēsous chosen_one.
   5 And to_remind you_all I_am_wishing, having_perceived you_all once all things, that Yēsous having_saved a_people out_of the_land of_Aiguptos/(Miʦərayim), in_the second place destroyed the ones not having_believed.
   6 And the_angels not having_kept the rule of_themselves, but having_left_behind their own dwelling, has_kept in_ eternal _bonds under darkness to the_judgment of_the_great day.
   7 As Sodoma/(Şədom) and Gomorra/('Amorāh), and the cities around them, having_fornicated in_a_similar manner to_these, and having_gone_away after different flesh, they_are_lying_before an_example of_ the_justice undergoing eternal _fire.
   8 Likewise however also these dreaming ones, are_defiling the_flesh on_one_hand, on_the_other_hand they_are_rejecting mastership, on_the_other_hand they_are_slandering glories.
   9 But Miⱪaēl/(Mīkā'ēl) the archangel, when disputing with_the devil, was_discussing about the body of_Mōsēs/(Mosheh), dared not to_bring_ a_judgment of_slander _against, but said, the_master might_give_rebuke to_you.
   10 But these as_much_as on_one_hand they_have_ not _known they_are_slandering, on_the_other_hand as_much_as naturally as illogical animals are_understanding, they_are_being_corrupted in these things.
   11 Woe to_them.
   Because they_were_gone in_the way of_Kain/(Qayin), and they_were_poured_out to_the deception of_Bala'am/(Bilə'ām) for_reward, and in_the controversy of_Kore/(Qoraḩ) they_perished.
   12 These are the ones in the love_feasts of_you_all, stains fearlessly feasting_with, shepherding themselves, waterless clouds being_carried_away by of_winds, unfruitful late_autumn trees having_died_off twice having_been_uprooted, 13 wild waves of_the_sea foaming_out the shame of_themselves, wandering stars, to_whom the darkness of_darkness has_been_kept to the_age.
   14 And Enōⱪ/(Ḩanōkə) the_seventh from Adam/('Ādām) prophesied also to_these, saying: see, the_master came among holy myriads of_him, 15 to_execute judgment against all, and to_rebuke all the ungodly concerning all their works of_ungodliness of_them, which they_acted_ungodly, and concerning all the hard things, of_which ungodly sinners spoke against him.
   16 These are grumblings complaining, following according_to the lusts of_them, and the mouth of_them is_speaking arrogant words, marvelling people for_reason_of benefit.
   17 But you_all, beloved, be_reminded of_the words having_been_previously_spoken by the ambassadors of_the master of_us, Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one/messiah, 18 that they_were_saying to_you_all: in the_last time will_be mockers following according_to the of_ungodliness lusts of_themselves.
   19 These are the ones dividing, soulish, not having the_spirit.
   20 But you_all beloved, building_ yourselves _up.
   in_the holiest faith of_you_all, praying in the_ holy _spirit.
   21 We_may_keep yourselves in the_love of_god, waiting_for the mercy of_the master of_us, Yēsous chosen_one, to eternal life.
   22 And on_one_hand be_showing_mercy to_some doubting, 23 some be_saving on_the_other_hand snatching out_of the_fire, and be_showing_mercy to_some with fear, hating even the clothes having_been_stained by the flesh.
   24 And to_the_ infallible _one being_able to_keep you_all, and to_stand blameless in_front_of the glory of_him, with exultation, 25 to_the_only god saviour of_us, through Yēsous chosen_one the master of_us, glory, majesty, dominion, and authority, before all the age, and now, and to all the ages.
   Truly.
