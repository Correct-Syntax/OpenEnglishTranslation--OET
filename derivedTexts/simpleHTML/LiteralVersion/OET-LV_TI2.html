2 Timotheos

1  Paulos, an_ambassador of_chosen_one/messiah Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a), by the_will of_god, according_to promise of_life in chosen_one Yēsous, 2 to_Timotheos beloved child: grace, mercy, peace, from god the_father and chosen_one Yēsous the master of_us.
   3 Thankfulness I_am_having to_god, unto_whom I_am_serving from my_parents with a_pure conscience, as unceasing I_am_having the for you mentioning, in the petitions of_me, night and day, 4 longing you to_see, having_remembered of_you of_the tears, in_order_that with_joy I_may_be_filled, 5 remembrance having_taken of_the in you unhypocritical faith, which dwelt first in the grandmother of_you, Lōis, and in_the mother of_you, Eunikē, and I_have_been_persuaded that also in you it_is.
   6 For which cause I_am_reminding you to_be_rekindling the gift of_god, which is in you by the laying_on of_the hands of_me.
   7 For not gave to_us god a_spirit of_fear, but of_power, and of_love, and of_sensibility.
   8 Therefore not may_be_ashamed the testimony of_the master of_us, nor me, the prisoner of_him, but suffer_together for_the gospel, according_to the_power of_god, 9 the one having_saved us, and having_called with_a_calling holy, not according_to the works of_us, but according_to his_own purpose and grace, having_been_given to_us in chosen_one Yēsous before times eternal, 10 but having_been_revealed now, by the appearing of_the saviour of_us, chosen_one Yēsous, having_nullified on_one_hand death, on_the_other_hand having_illuminated life and indestructibility through the gospel, 11 to which was_appointed I a_proclaimer, and an_ambassador, and a_teacher.
   12 For which cause also these things I_am_suffering, but not I_am_being_ashamed, for I_have_known in_whom I_have_believed, and I_have_been_persuaded that powerful he_is, the deposit of_me to_guard for that day.
   13 The_pattern be_having of_being_sound words, which from me you_heard, in faith and love in chosen_one Yēsous.
   14 The good deposit keep, by the_spirit holy dwelling in us.
   15 You_have_known this, that were_turned_away from_me all who are in Asia, of_whom is Fugelos and Hermogenēs.
   16 Might_give mercy the master to_the of_Onēsiforos household, because often me he_refreshed, and the chain of_me not was_ashamed, 17 but having_become in Ɽōmē, earnestly he_sought me and found 18 (might_give to_him the master, to_find mercy from the_master in that day), and as_much_as in Efesos he_served, better you are_knowing.
  2  Therefore you, child of_me, be_being_strengthened in the grace in chosen_one/messiah Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a).
   2 And the things you_heard from me through many witnesses, these entrust to_faithful people, who worthy will_be also others to_teach.
   3 Suffer_together as a_good soldier of_chosen_one Yēsous.
   4 No_one soldiering is_being_entangled with_the of_living affairs, in_order_that to_the one having_enlisted him he_may_bring_pleasure.
   5 And if also may_be_competing anyone, not is_being_crowned, if not lawfully he_may_compete.
   6 The laboring farmer it_is_fitting first of_the fruits to_be_partaking.
   7 Be_understanding what I_am_saying, for will_be_giving to_you the master insight in all things.
   8 Be_remembering Yēsous chosen_one, having_been_raised from the_dead, of the_seed of_Dauid/(Dāvid), according_to the gospel of_me, 9 in which I_am_suffering_hardship unto bonds as a_criminal.
   But the word of_god not has_been_bound.
   10 Because_of this, all things I_am_enduring because_of the chosen ones, in_order_that also they of_salvation may_obtain in chosen_one Yēsous with glory eternal.
   11 Trustworthy is the saying: for if we_died_together with_him, also we_will_be_living_together with_him, 12 if we_are_enduring, also we_may_reign him, if we_will_be_disowning him, also_that one will_be_disowning us, 13 if we_are_disbelieving, that one faithful is_remaining, for to_disown himself not he_is_able.
   14 These things be_reminding them, testifying before god, not he_is_quibbling against nothing useful, to the_ruin of_the ones hearing.
   15 Be_earnest yourself approved to_present to_god, a_worker unashamed, cutting_straight the word of_truth.
   16 And profane empty_babblings be_avoiding, on for more they_will_be_progressing of_ungodliness, 17 and the word of_them as gangrene spreading will_be_having, of_whom is Humenaios, and Filētos, 18 who concerning the truth deviated, saying the the_resurrection already to_have_become, and are_overturning the of_some faith.
   19 The however solid foundation of_god has_stood, having seal this: knew the_master the ones being of_him, and: let_withdraw from unrighteousness everyone naming the name of_the_master.
   20 In and a_great house, not there_is only vessels golden and silver, but also wooden and earthen, and some on_one_hand for honor, on_the_other_hand some to dishonor.
   21 Therefore if anyone may_clean_out himself from these things, he_will_be a_vessel for honor, having_been_sanctified useful to_the master, for every work good having_been_prepared.
   22 And youthful lusts be_fleeing, but be_pursuing righteousness, faith, love, peace, with all the ones calling_on the master out_of pure a_heart.
   23 And foolish and uneducated debates be_refusing, having_known that they_are_bearing quarrels.
   24 And the_slave of_the_master not it_is_fitting to_be_quarrelling, but gentle to_be toward all, teaching, bearing_evil, 25 in gentleness disciplining the ones opposing, perhaps may_give to_them god repentance to a_knowledge of_the_truth, 26 and they_may_recover_sobriety out_of the of_the devil snare, having_been_caught by him, in_order that of_that one the_will.
  3  But this be_knowing, that in the_last days will_be_presenting times dangerous.
   2 For will_be people selfish, money_lovers, braggers, proud, slanderous, to_their_parents unpersuadable, ungrateful, unholy, 3 unloving, irreconcilable, diabolical, intemperate, untamed, unwholesome, 4 betrayers, reckless, having_been_conceited, lovers_of_pleasure rather than lovers_of_god, 5 having an_appearance of_devoutness, but the power of_it having_disowned, and these be_turning_from.
   6 Out_of for these, are the ones slipping_in into houses, and making_captive silly_women, having_been_heaped with_sins, being_led by_lusts various, 7 always learning, and never to a_knowledge of_the_truth to_come being_able.
   8 ˱By˲_which and manner Yannēs and Yambrēs resisted against_Mōsēs/(Mosheh), thus also these are_resisting against_the truth, people having_been_depraved the_mind, unqualified concerning the faith.
   9 But not they_will_be_progressing to more, for the folly of_them evident will_be to_all, as also the folly of_those became.
   10 And you followed of_me with_the teaching, the conduct, the purpose, the faith, the patience, the love, the endurance, 11 the persecutions, the sufferings, such_as to_me became in Antioⱪeia, in Ikonion, in Lustra, such_as persecutions I_bore_under, and_yet out_of all things, me rescued the master.
   12 Also and all the ones wanting to_be_living devoutly in chosen_one/messiah Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) will_be_being_persecuted.
   13 But evil people and swindlers will_be_progressing to the worse, deceiving and being_strayed.
   14 And you be_remaining in what things you_learned and you_were_convinced_of, having_known from whom_all you_learned them, 15 and that from a_baby the the_sacred writings you_have_known, being_able you to_make_wise to salvation through faith in chosen_one Yēsous.
   16 Every scripture is god-breathed and beneficial for teaching, for rebuke, for correction, for discipline in righteousness, 17 in_order_that complete may_be the of_god person, toward every work good having_been_finished_out.
  4  I_am_testifying before god and chosen_one Yēsous, the one going to_be_judging the_living and the_dead, both the appearing of_him, and the kingdom of_him: 2 proclaim the word, stand_by conveniently, ˱and˲_unseasonably, rebuke, give_rebuke, exhort, with all patience and teaching.
   3 For there_will_be a_time when of_the being_sound teaching not they_will_be_tolerating, but according_to own desires, to_themselves they_will_be_accumulating teachers, being_tickled by_the ear, 4 and from on_one_hand the truth hearing they_will_be_turning_away, on_the_other_hand to myths they_will_be_being_turned_away.
   5 But you be_being_sober in all things, suffer_hardship, as a_good soldier of_chosen_one Yēsous the_work do of_a_gospel_preacher, the service of_you fully_assure.
   6 For I already am_being_poured_out, and the time of_the departure of_me has_approached.
   7 good fight I_have_fought, the course I_have_finished, the faith I_have_kept.
   8 Furthermore is_laid_away for_me the of_righteousness crown, which will_be_giving_back to_me the master in, that day, the righteous judge, not and only to_me, but also to_all the ones having_loved the appearing of_him.
   9 Be_earnest to_come to me quickly, 10 For Dēmas me was_abandoning, having_loved the present age, and was_gone to Thessalonikē, Krēskēs to NOT_Galilaia_NOT, Titos to Dalmatia.
   11 Loukas is only with me.
   Markos having_taken_up, be_bringing him with yourself, for he_is to_me useful for the_service.
   12 But Tuⱪikos I_sent_out to Efesos.
   13 The cloak that I_left_behind in Trōias with Karpos, on_coming be_bringing, and the scrolls, most_of_all the parchments.
   14 Alexandros the coppersmith, great to_me harm displayed, will_be_giving_back to_him the master according_to the works of_him, 15 whom also you be_guarding, for exceedingly he_resisted against_our words.
   16 In first of_me defense, no_one with_me appeared, but all me abandoned, not to_them it_might_have_reckoned.
   17 But the master by_me stood_by, and strengthened me, in_order_that through me the proclamation may_be_fully_assured, and may_hear all the pagans, and I_was_rescued out_of the_mouth of_the_lion.
   18 Will_be_rescuing me the master from every work evil, and he_will_be_saving into the kingdom of_him heavenly, to_whom is the glory to the ages of_the ages.
   Truly.
   19 Greet Priska and Akulas, and the of_Onēsiforos house.
   20 Erastos remained in Korinthos, But Trofimos I_left_behind in Milētos ailing.
   21 Be_earnest before winter to_come.
   Is_greeting you Euboulos, and Poudēs, and Linos, and Klaudia, and the brothers all.
   22 The master Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) with the spirit of_you be.
   grace be with you_all.
