1  What was from the_beginning, what we_have_heard, what we_have_seen with_the eyes of_us, what we_saw, and the hands of_us touched, concerning the word of_life, 2 and the life was_revealed, and we_have_seen, and we_are_testifying, and we_are_reporting to_you_all, the life eternal, which was with the father, and was_revealed to_us, 3 what we_have_seen, and we_have_heard, we_are_reporting also to_you_all, in_order_that also you_all fellowship may_be_having with us, also and fellowship our is with the father, and with the son of_him, Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one/messiah.
4 And these things are_writing we, in_order_that the joy of_us may_be having_been_fulfilled.
5 And is this the message which we_have_heard from him and we_are_declaring to_you_all, that god light is, and darkness in him, not is not_one.
6 If we_may_say that fellowship we_are_having with him, and_yet in the darkness we_may_be_walking, we_are_lying and not we_are_practicing the truth.
7 But if in the light we_may_be_walking, as he is in the light, fellowship we_are_having with one_another, and the blood of_Yēsous the son of_him, is_cleansing us from all sin.
8 If we_may_say that sin not we_are_having, ourselves we_are_deceiving and the truth not is in us.
9 If we_may_be_confessing the sins of_us, faithful he_is and righteous, in_order_that he_may_forgive to_us our sins, and may_cleanse us from all unrighteousness.
10 If we_may_say that not we_have_sinned, a_liar we_are_making him, and the word of_him not is in us.
2  Little_children of_me, these things I_am_writing to_you_all, in_order_that not you_may_sin.
And if anyone may_sin, an_advocate we_are_having with the father, Yēsous chosen_one the_righteous, 2 and he the_atonement is for the sins of_us, not for and our sins only, but also for all the world.
3 And by this we_are_knowing that we_have_known him, if the commandments of_him we_may_be_keeping.
4 The one saying, I_have_known him, and the commandments of_him not keeping, a_liar is, and in this one the truth not is, 5 but whoever may_be_keeping of_him the word, truly in this one the love of_god has_been_perfected.
By this we_are_knowing that in him we_are: 6 the one saying in him to_be_remaining, ought as that one walked, also himself to_be_walking.

The commandment old is the word that you_all_heard.

9 The one saying in the light to_be, and the brother of_him hating, in the darkness is until now.
10 The one loving the brother of_him, in the light is_remaining, and offense not is.
in him.
