Filippēsios

1  Paulos and Timotheos, slaves of_chosen_one/messiah Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a), to_all the holy ones in chosen_one Yēsous being in Filippou, with the_overseers and servants: 2 grace to_you_all and peace from god the_father of_us and the_master Yēsous chosen_one.
   3 I_am_giving_thanks to_the god of_me on every mentioning of_you_all, 4 always in every petition of_me for all of_you_all, with joy the petition making, 5 for the fellowship of_you_all in the gospel, from the first day until the time now, 6 having_persuaded same thing this, that the one having_begun in you_all a_work good, will_be_accomplishing it until the_day of_chosen_one Yēsous, 7 as it_is right for_me this to_be_thinking about all of_you_all, because_of that to_be_having me in my heart you_all, in both the bonds of_me, and in the defense and confirmation of_the gospel, fellow-partakers with_me of_the grace all you_all being.
   8 For witness of_me god is, how I_am_longing_for all you_all in the_compassions of_chosen_one Yēsous.
   9 And this I_am_praying, that the love of_you_all still more and more may_be_abounding in knowledge and all insight, 10 in_order that to_be_approving you_all the things carrying_value, in_order_that you_all_may_be sincere and inoffensive to the_day of_chosen_one, 11 having_been_filled with_the_fruit of_righteousness through Yēsous chosen_one, to the_glory and praise of_god.
   12 And to_be_knowing you_all, I_am_wishing, brothers, that the things concerning me, more to the_progress of_the gospel has_come, 13 so_as the bonds of_me manifest in chosen_one to_become, in all the residence_of_the_governor and to_the rest all, 14 and more of_the brothers in the_master having_trusted by_the_bonds of_me, more_abundantly to_be_daring fearlessly the word of_god to_be_speaking.
   15 Some on_one_hand even because_of envy and strife, on_the_other_hand some also because_of goodwill the chosen_one are_proclaiming.
   16 The ones on_the_other_hand out_of selfish_ambition the chosen_one are_proclaiming, not purely supposing tribulation to_be_raising to_the bonds of_me.
   17 The ones indeed from love having_known that for defense of_the gospel I_am_appointing, 18 for what.
   Except that in_every manner, whether in_under_pretense or in_truth, chosen_one/messiah is_being_proclaimed, and in this I_am_rejoicing, but also I_will_be_being_elated.
   19 I_have_known and that this for_me will_be_turning_out to salvation through the of_you_all petition, and of_the_supply of_the spirit of_Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one, 20 according_to the eager_expectation and hope of_me, that in nothing I_will_be_being_ashamed, but in all boldness, as always also now, will_be_being_magnified chosen_one in the body of_me, whether by life or by death.
   21 For to_me to_be_living is chosen_one, and to_die_off is gain.
   22 But if to_be_living in the_flesh, this for_me is the_fruit of_work.
   And what I_may_chose not I_am_making_known.
   23 I_am_being_pressed and by the two, the desire having in_order that to_depart and with chosen_one to_be, for by_much more better, 24 but to_be_remaining_on in the flesh, more_necessary because_of you_all.
   25 And this having_persuaded, I_have_known that I_will_be_remaining and I_will_be_continuing_with with_all you_all for the of_you_all progress and joy of_the faith, 26 in_order_that the boast of_you_all may_be_abounding in chosen_one Yēsous in me, through my coming again to you_all.
   27 Only worthily of_the gospel of_the chosen_one be_living_as_citizen, in_order_that whether having_come and having_seen you_all, or being_absent, I_may_be_hearing the things concerning you_all, that you_all_are_standing_firm in one with_one, spirit heart contending_together for_the faith of_the gospel, 28 and not being_frightened in nothing by the ones opposing, which is to_them a_sign of_destruction, but to_you_all of_salvation, and this from god, 29 because to_you_all it_was_granted concerning chosen_one, not only on him to_be_believing, but also concerning him to_be_suffering, 30 the same struggle having, such_as you_all_saw in me, and now are_hearing in me.
  2  If therefore any exhortation in chosen_one, if any consolation of_love, if any fellowship of_the_spirit, if anyone has compassions and compassions, 2 fulfill of_me the joy, in_order_that the same thing you_all_may_be_thinking, the same love having, united_in_soul, the one thing thinking.
   3 Nothing do according_to selfish_ambition nor according_to vain_conceit, but in_humility one_another considering the ones being_above of_themselves, 4 not the things of_themselves each looking_out, but also the things of_others each.
   5 This be_thinking in us, which also in chosen_one/messiah Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) is, 6 who in the_form of_god being, not advantage considered to_be equal with_god, 7 but himself emptied, the_form of_a_slave having_taken, in the_likeness of_humans having_become.
   And in_form having_been_found as a_human, 8 he_humbled himself, having_become obedient unto death, and the_death of_the_cross.
   9 Therefore also god him highly_exalted, and granted to_him the name above every name, 10 in_order_that at the name of_Yēsous, every knee may_bow of_heavenly and earthly and subterranean realms, 11 and every tongue may_confess that the_master is Yēsous is chosen_one, to the_glory of_god the_father.
   12 So_then, beloved of_me, as always you_all_submitted, not as in the presence of_me only, but now by_much more in the absence of_me, with fear and trembling the of_yourselves salvation be_producing.
   13 For god is the one working in you_all, both to_be_willing, and to_be_working, for his good_pleasure.
   14 All things be_doing without grumblings and speculations, 15 in_order_that you_all_may_become blameless and innocent, children of_god blameless in_the_midst of_a_generation crooked and having_been_perverted, among whom you_all_are_shining as lights in the_world, 16 the_word of_life holding_up to a_boast to_me in the_day of_chosen_one, because not in vain I_ran, nor in vain I_labored, 17 but if even I_am_being_poured_out on the sacrifice and ministry of_the faith of_you_all, I_am_rejoicing and I_am_rejoicing_with with_all you_all.
   18 And the same also you_all be_rejoicing and be_rejoicing_with with_me.
   19 But I_am_hoping in the_master Yēsous, Timotheos quickly to_send to_you_all, in_order_that also_I may_be_encouraging, having_known the things concerning you_all.
   20 For no_one I_am_having kindred-spirit, who genuinely the things concerning you_all will_be_caring_for.
   21 The ones for all the things of_themselves are_seeking, not the things of_Yēsous chosen_one.
   22 But the provenness of_him you_all_are_knowing, that as unto_a_father a_child serves, with me he_served in the gospel.
   23 This one therefore on_one_hand I_am_hoping to_send, when I_may_look_out the things concerning me immediately, 24 on_the_other_hand I_have_persuaded in the_master that also myself quickly I_will_be_coming.
   25 But necessary I_considered, Epafroditos, the brother and fellow-worker and fellow-soldier of_me, and of_you_all ambassador and minister of_the need of_me, to_send to you_all, 26 since longing_for he_was for_all for_you_all, and distressing because you_all_heard that he_ailed.
   27 For also he_ailed nearly to_death, but god showed_mercy_to him, not and him only, but also me, in_order_that not sorrow on sorrow I_may_have.
   28 Therefore more_earnestly I_sent him, in_order_that having_seen him again, you_all_may_be_elated and_I less_anxious may_be.
   29 Therefore be_accepting him in the_master with all joy, and such honored ones be_having, 30 because because_of the work of_chosen_one/messiah unto death he_neared, having_risked with_his life, in_order_that he_may_fulfill_up the of_you_all lacking, of_the toward me ministry.
  3  For_the rest, brothers of_me, be_rejoicing in the_master.
   The same things to_be_writing to_you_all, to_me is on_one_hand not reluctant, on_the_other_hand for_you_all is secure.
   2 Be_watching_out for_the dogs, be_watching_out for_the evil workers, be_watching_out for_the mutilation.
   3 For we are the circumcision, the ones in_the_spirit of_god worshiping and boasting in chosen_one Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a), and not in the_flesh having_trusted, 4 although I having confidence even in the_flesh.
   If any is_supposing other to_have_trusted in the_flesh, I more: 5 in_circumcision the_eight_day of the_descent of_Israēl/(Yisərā'ēl) of_the_tribe of_Beniamin/(Binəyāmīn), a_Hebraios of Hebraios, according_to the_law a_Farisaios_sect_member, 6 according_to zeal persecuting the assembly, according_to righteousness which is by the_law having_become blameless.
   7 which things was to_me gain, these I_have_considered because_of the chosen_one loss.
   8 But indeed_rather also I_am_considering all things loss to_be, because_of that being_above the knowledge of_chosen_one Yēsous the master of_me, because_of whom the things all I_was_lost and I_am_considering them rubbish, in_order_that chosen_one I_may_gain 9 and may_be_found in him, not having my own righteousness of the_law, but the righteousness through faith in_chosen_one, the of god righteousness based on faith, 10 to_know him, and the power of_the resurrection of_him, and fellowship of_the_sufferings of_him, being_conformed to_the death of_him, 11 if somehow I_may_attain to the resurrection from the_dead.
   12 Not that already I_received it, or already I_have_been_perfected, but I_am_pursuing, if also I_may_grasp, in that also I_was_grasped by chosen_one Yēsous.
   13 Brothers, I myself not am_reckoning to_have_grasped it, but I_do one thing: the things on_one_hand behind forgetting, on_the_other_hand to_the things ahead stretching_out, 14 to the_goal I_am_pursuing for the prize of_the upward calling of_god in chosen_one/messiah Yēsous.
   15 Therefore as_many_as mature are, this we_may_be_thinking, and if anything differently you_all_are_thinking, even this god to_you_all will_be_revealing.
   16 However to what we_attained, by_the same thing to_be_aligning.
   17 Fellow-imitators of_me be_becoming, brothers, and be_observing the ones thus walking, as you_all_are_having for_an_example us.
   18 For many are_walking, of_whom often I_was_telling to_you_all, and now even weeping, I_am_telling you, enemies of_the cross of_the chosen_one, 19 whose end is destruction, of_whose god is their belly, and their glory is in the shame of_them, the ones the earthly things thinking.
   20 For of_us the citizenship in the_heavens is_being, from which also a_saviour we_are_eagerly_waiting, the_master Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one, 21 who will_be_adapting the body of_humiliation of_us, similar to_the body of_the glory of_him, according_to the working of_the thing to_be_able him, even to_subject to_him the things all.
  4  So_then, brothers of_me beloved and longed for, joy and crown of_me, thus be_standing_firm in the_master, beloved.
   2 Euodia I_am_exhorting, and Suntuⱪē I_am_exhorting, the same thing to_be_thinking in the_master.
   3 Yes, I_am_asking also you, genuine companion, be_giving_help to_them, who in the gospel contended_together with_me, with both Klēmēs and the rest of_the_fellow-workers of_me, whose names are in the_scroll of_life.
   4 Be_rejoicing in the_master always, again I_will_be_saying, be_rejoicing.
   5 The gentle of_you_all let_be_known to_all people.
   The master near.
   6 Nothing be_worrying, but in everything, by_prayer and petition with thanksgiving, the requests of_you_all let_be_being_made_known to god.
   7 And the peace of_god being_above all mind, will_be_guarding the hearts of_you_all and the thinking of_you_all in chosen_one Yēsous.
   8 For_the rest, brothers, as_much_as is true, as_much_as dignified, as_much_as right, as_much_as pure, as_much_as lovely, as_much_as admirable, if any virtue, and if any praise, these things be_reckoning.
   9 What both you_all_learned and you_all_received, both you_all_heard and you_all_saw, in me, these things be_practicing, and the god of_peace will_be with you_all.
   10 But I_was_elated in the_master greatly, that now once you_all_revived for me to_be_thinking, to which also you_all_were_thinking, but you_all_were_lacking_season.
   11 Not that concerning poverty I_am_speaking, for I learned in whatever situations I_am content to_be.
   12 I_have_known both to_be_being_humbled, I_have_known and to_be_abounding.
   In everything and in all things I_have_been_enlightened, both to_be_being_satisfied and to_be_hungering, both to_be_abounding and to_be_being_deficient.
   13 All things I_am_being_strong in the one strengthening me.
   14 However well you_all_did, having_participated of_me in_the tribulation.
   15 But have_known also you_all, Filippēsios, that in the_beginning of_the gospel, when I_came_out from Makedonia, not_one with_me assembly shared in the_matter of_giving and receiving, except you_all only.
   16 Because even in Thessalonikē, both once and twice, for the need to_me you_all_sent.
   17 Not that I_am_seeking_after the gift, but I_am_seeking_after the fruit abounding to the_account of_you_all.
   18 But I_am_receiving_fully all things, and I_am_abounding, I_have_been_filled, having_received from Epafroditos the things from you_all, an_aroma of_a_sweet_fragrance, a_sacrifice acceptable, well_pleasing to_god.
   19 But the god of_me will_be_filling all the_need of_you_all, according_to the riches of_him in glory in chosen_one/messiah Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a).
   20 And to_the god and father of_us be the glory to the ages of_the ages.
   Truly.
   21 Greet every holy one in chosen_one Yēsous, are_greeting you_all the with me brothers, 22 are_greeting you_all all the holy ones, and most_of_all the ones from the of_Kaisar house.
   23 The grace of_the master Yēsous chosen_one be with the spirit of_you_all.
   Truly.
