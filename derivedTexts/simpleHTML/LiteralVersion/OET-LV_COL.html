Colossians

1  Paulos, an_ambassador of_messiah Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) by the_will of_god, and Timotheos our brother, 2 to_the in Colosse holy ones, and faithful brothers in messiah: grace to_you_all and peace from god the_father of_us and the_master Yēsous messiah.
   3 We_are_giving_thanks to_god the_father of_the master of_us Yēsous messiah, always for you_all praying, 4 having_heard the faith of_you_all in messiah Yēsous, and the love that you_all_are_having toward all the holy ones, 5 because_of the hope lying_away for_you_all in the heavens, which you_all_previously_heard in the word of_the truth, the gospel, 6 the one being_present to you_all, as also in all the world, is bearing_fruit and being_grown, as also among you_all, from which day you_all_heard and knew the grace of_god in truth; 7 as you_all_learned from Epafras, the beloved fellow-slave of_us, who is faithful for us a_servant of_the messiah, 8 the one also having_made_evident to_us the of_you_all love in the_spirit.
   9 Because_of this also we, from which day we_heard it, not are_ceasing for you_all praying and requesting, in_order_that you_all_may_be_filled with_the knowledge of_the will of_him in all wisdom and insight spiritual, 10 to_walk worthily of_the master in all pleasing, in every work good bearing_fruit, and being_grown in_the knowledge of_god, 11 with all power being_enabled, according_to the power of_the glory of_him, to all endurance and patience with joy, 12 giving_thanks together_with the father, the one having_qualified you_all for the portion of_the lot of_the holy ones in the light, 13 who rescued us from the power of_darkness, and redirected us into the kingdom of_the son of_the love of_him, 14 in whom we_are_having redemption, the forgiveness of_sins; 15 who is the_image of_the god invisible, the_firstborn of_all creation, 16 because by him was_created the things all in the heavens and on the earth, the visible and the invisible, whether thrones, or lordships, or rulers, or authorities; the things all by him and for him has_been_created.
   17 And he is before all things, and the things all in him has_consisted.
   18 And he is the head of_the body, the assembly, who is the beginning, firstborn from the dead, in_order_that may_become in all things he being_first, 19 because in him he_took_pleasure all the fullness to_dwell, 20 and by him to_reconcile the things all to himself, having_made_peace by the blood of_the cross of_him through him, whether the things on earth, or the things in the heavens.
   21 And you_all once being having_been_estranged and enemies in_mind in your works evil, 22 but now you_all_have_been_reconciled in the body of_the flesh of_him, through his death, to_present you_all holy, and blameless, and unindictable, in_front_of him– 23 if surely you_all_are_continuing_on in_the_faith, having_been_established and firm, and not being_shifted from the hope of_the gospel which you_all_heard, having_been_proclaimed in all creation under heaven, of_which became I Paulos a_servant.
   24 Now I_am_rejoicing in the sufferings for you_all, and I_am_filling_up the lacking things of_the tribulations of_the messiah in the flesh of_me for the body of_him, which is the assembly, 25 of_which became I a_servant, according_to the management of_god, having_been_given to_me for you_all to_fulfill the word of_god, 26 the mystery having_been_hidden_away from the ages and from the generations, but now was_revealed to_the holy ones of_him, 27 to_whom willed god, to_make_known what is the riches of_the glory of_mystery this among the pagans, which is messiah in you_all, the hope of_glory; 28 whom we are_proclaiming, admonishing every person and teaching every person in all wisdom, in_order_that we_may_present every person perfect in messiah.
   29 To which also I_am_laboring, striving according_to the working of_him, working in me in power.
  2  For I_am_wanting you_all to_have_known, how_much a_struggle I_am_having for you_all and the ones in Laodikeia, and as_many_as not have_seen the face of_me in the_flesh, 2 in_order_that may_be_comforted the hearts of_them, having_been_held_together in love, and to all the_riches of_the full_assurance of_insight, to the_knowledge of_the mystery of_god, messiah, 3 in whom are all the treasures of_wisdom and of_knowledge secret.
   4 This I_am_saying, in_order_that no_one you_all may_be_deluding by persuade_speech.
   5 For if also in_the flesh I_am_being_absent, but in_spirit with you_all I_am, rejoicing and seeing of_you_all the order, and the firmness of_the in messiah faith of_you_all.
   6 Therefore as you_all_received messiah Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) the master, in him be_walking, 7 having_been_rooted and being_built_up in him, and being_confirmed in_the faith, as you_all_were_taught, abounding in thanksgiving.
   8 Be_watching_out, lest anyone you_all will_be taking_captive through philosophy and empty seduction, according_to the tradition of_humans, according_to the principles of_the world, and not according_to messiah.
   9 Because in him is_dwelling all the fullness of_the deity bodily, 10 and you_all_are in him having_been_fulfilled, he is the head of_all rule and authority, 11 in whom also you_all_were_circumcised with_circumcision not_hand_made, in the disarmament of_the body of_the flesh, by the circumcision of_the messiah, 12 having_been_buried_with with_him in washing, in which also you_all_were_raised_up_with him, through the faith in_the working of_god, having_raised him from the_dead.
   13 And you_all dead being in the transgressions and in_the uncircumcision of_the flesh of_you_all, he_made_alive_together us with him, having_forgiven to_us all our transgressions, 14 having_wiped_out the against us handwriting in_the decrees, which was hostile to_us, and it has_taken_away out_of the midst, having_nailed it to_the cross; 15 having_disarmed the rulers and the authorities, and he_exposed them in public, having_triumphed over_them in him.
   16 Therefore not anyone you_all let_be_judging in food, and in drink, or in regard of_a_feast, or new_moon, or sabbaths, 17 which is a_shadow of_the things coming, but the body is of_the messiah.
   18 No_one you_all let_be_disqualifying, willing in humility, and the_worship of_the angels, which he_has_seen detailing, vainly being_arrogant by the mind of_the flesh of_him, 19 and not taking_hold_of the head, from whom all the body by its joints and ligaments being_supplied and being_held_together, is_growing with_the growth of_god.
   20 If you_all_died_off with messiah from the principles of_the world, why as_though living in the_world you_all_are_being_indoctrinated: 21 Not you_may_touch.
   Not you_may_taste.
   Not you_may_touch.
   22 Which is all to corruption in_the depletion, according_to the commandments and teachings of_humans.
   23 Which things is an_account indeed having of_wisdom in personal_religion and humility, severity of_the_body, not in honor any against the_indulgence of_the flesh.
  3  Therefore if you_all_were_raised_up_with with_the messiah, the things above be_seeking, where the messiah is, at the_right hand of_god sitting.
   2 The things above be_thinking, not the things on the earth.
   3 For you_all_died_off, and the life of_you_all has_been_hidden with the messiah in god.
   4 Whenever the messiah may_be_revealed, the life of_you_all, then also you_all with him will_be_being_revealed in glory.
   5 Therefore make_dead your members which are on the earth: sexual_immorality, impurity, passion, desire evil, and greediness, which is idolatry, 6 because_of which things is_coming the severe_anger of_god, 7 among whom also you_all walked once when you_all_were_living in these things.
   8 But now put_away also you_all the things all: severe_anger, rage, malice, slander, profanity out_of the mouth of_you_all.
   9 Not be_lying to one_another, having_disarmed the old personal with the practices of_him, 10 and having_dressed_in the new man, being_renewed in knowledge, according_to the_image of_the one having_created him, 11 where not there_is Hellēn and Jew, circumcision and uncircumcision, foreigner, Skuthēs, slave, free, but all things and in all is messiah.
   12 Therefore dress_in, as the_chosen of_god holy ones and having_been_loved, hearts of_compassion, kindness, humility, gentleness, patience, 13 tolerating of_one_another and forgiving to_yourselves, if anyone against anyone may_be_having a_complaint; as also the master forgave to_you_all, thus also you_all forgive.
   14 Over and all these things, put_on love, which is the_bond of_perfection.
   15 And the peace of_the messiah let_be_deciding in the hearts of_you_all, to which also you_all_were_called in body; and thankful be_becoming.
   16 The word of_the messiah let_be_dwelling_in in you_all richly in all wisdom, teaching and admonishing yourselves in_psalms, hymns, songs spiritual, with thankfulness singing in the hearts of_you_all to_the to_god.
   17 And everything, whatever anything you_all_may_be_doing in word or in work, all in the_name of_the_master Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) do, giving_thanks to_god the_father through him.
   18 wives, be_being_subjected to_your husbands, as was_fitting in the_master.
   19 husbands, be_loving your wives, and not be_being_embittered toward them.
   20 children, be_submitting to_your parents in all things, for this well_pleasing is in the_master.
   21 fathers, not be_provoking the children of_you_all, in_order_that not they_may_be_losing_heart.
   22 slaves, be_submitting in all things to_your according_to flesh masters, not with eyeservice, as men_pleasing, but in sincerity of_heart, revering the master.
   23 Whatever you_all_may_be_doing, with /your/_heart be_working, as to_the master not to_people, 24 having_known that from the_master you_all_will_be_taking_back the repaying of_your inheritance.
   For_the master messiah you_all_are_serving.
   25 The one for doing_wrong, will_be_receiving_back what he_did_wrong, and not there_is favoritism.
  4  masters, what is righteous and equality to_your slaves be_bringing_about, having_known that also you_all are_having a_Master in heaven.
   2 in_prayer be_persevering, watching in it with thanksgiving, 3 praying together also for us, in_order_that god may_open_up to_us a_door for_the word, to_speak the mystery of_the messiah, because_of which also I_have_been_bound, 4 in_order_that I_may_reveal it, as it_is_fitting me to_speak.
   5 In wisdom be_walking toward the ones outside, the time redeeming.
   6 the_speech of_you_all always in grace let_be, with_salt having_been_seasoned, to_have_known how it_is_fitting you_all to_one each to_be_answering.
   7 The things concerning me all will_be_making_known to_you_all Tuⱪikos, the beloved brother, and faithful servant, and fellow-slave in the_master, 8 whom I_sent to you_all for same thing this, in_order_that you_all_may_know the things concerning you_all, and he_may_comfort the hearts of_you_all, 9 with Onesimus, the faithful and beloved brother, who is of you_all.
   All things to_you_all they_will_be_making_known the things here.
   10 Is_greeting you_all Aristarⱪos, the fellow-prisoner of_me, and Markos, the cousin of_Barnabas (concerning whom you_all_received commandments, if he_may_come to you_all, receive him), 11 and Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a), the one being_called Youstos, the ones being of the_circumcision these are, the_only fellow-workers for the kingdom of_god, who were_become to_me an_encouragement.
   12 Is_greeting you_all Epafras, who is of you_all, a_slave of_messiah Yēsous, always striving for you_all in the prayers, in_order_that you_all_may_be_stood mature and having_been_fully_assured in all the_will of_god.
   13 For I_am_testifying to_him that he_is_having great distress for you_all, and the ones in Laodikeia, and the ones in Hierapolis.
   14 Is_greeting you_all Loukas, the physician beloved, and Dēmas.
   15 Greet the in Laodikeia brothers, and Numfas, and the in the_house of_them assembly.
   16 And whenever may_be_read among you_all my letter, make that also in the of_the_Laodikeians assembly it_may_be_read, and the letter from Laodikeia that also you_all may_read.
   17 And say to_Arⱪippos: Be_watching_out to_the service that you_received in the_master, in_order_that it you_may_be_fulfilling.
   18 The greeting is by_my own hand, of_Paulos.
   Be_remembering of_me about_the bonds.
   grace be with you_all.
