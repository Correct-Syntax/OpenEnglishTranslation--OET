1  The_scroll of_the_birth of_Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one/messiah, son of_Dauid/(Dāvid), son of_Abra'am/('Avərāhām): 2 Abra'am bore Isa'ak/(Yiʦəḩāq), and Isa'ak bore Yakōb/(Ya'aqov), and Yakōb bore Youda/(Yəhūdāh) and the brothers of_him, 3 And Youda bore Fares/(Fereʦ) and Zara/(Zeraḩ) of Thamar/(Tāmār), And Fares bore Hesrōm/(Ḩeʦərōn), And Hesrōm bore Aram/(Rām), 4 And Aram bore Aminadab/('Ammiynādāv), And Aminadab bore Na'assōn/(Naḩəshōn), And Na'assōn bore Salmōn/(Saləmōn), 5 And Salmōn bore Bo'oz/(Bo'az) of Ɽaⱪab/(Rāḩāv), And Bo'oz bore Yōbēd/Ōbēd/('Ōvēd) of Ɽouth/(Rūt), And Yōbēd bore Yessai/(Yishay), 6 And Yessai bore Dauid the king.

16 Then Hērōdēs, having_seen that he_was_mocked by the wise_men, was_enraged exceedingly and having_sent_out, he_killed all the boys in Bēthle'em and in all the regions of_it from two_year and lower, according_to the time which he_ascertained from the wise_men.
