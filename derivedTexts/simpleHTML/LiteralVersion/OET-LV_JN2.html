2 G‹Yōannēs›G

1  The elder, to_the_chosen mistress and the children of_her, whom I am_loving in truth (and not I only, but also all the ones having_known the truth), 2 because_of the truth remaining in us, and with us will_be to the age: 3 will_be with us grace, mercy, peace, from god the_father and from G‹H‹Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a)›H›G chosen_one/messiah, the son of_the father, in truth and love.
   4 I_was_elated exceedingly that I_have_found some of the children of_you walking in truth, as the_commandment we_received from the father.
   5 And now I_am_asking you, mistress, not as a_commandment new writing to_you, but what we_were_having from the_beginning, that we_may_be_loving one_another.
   6 And this is love: that we_may_be_walking according_to the commandments of_him.
   This the commandment is, as you_all_heard from the_beginning, that in same you_all_may_be_walking.
   7 Because many deceivers came_out into the world, the ones not confessing G‹H‹Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a)›H›G chosen_one/messiah coming in flesh.
   This one is the deceiver and the false_messiah.
   8 Be_seeing yourselves that not you_all_may_lose what things you_did, but a_reward full you_all_may_take_back.
   9 Everyone going_beyond and not remaining in the teaching of_the chosen_one/messiah, god not is_having, the one remaining in the teaching, this one both the father and the son is_having.
   10 If anyone is_coming to you_all, and this teaching not is_bringing, not be_receiving him into your_house, and to_be_rejoicing to_him not be_saying, 11 the one for saying to_him to_be_rejoicing is_sharing in_the works of_him evil.
   12 Many things having to_you_all to_be_writing, not I_was_counseled through paper and ink, but I_am_hoping to_become to you_all, and mouth to mouth to_speak, in_order_that the joy of_you_all having_been_fulfilled may_be.
   13 Is_greeting you the children of_the sister of_you chosen.
