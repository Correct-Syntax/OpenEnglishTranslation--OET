1  Paulos, an_ambassador of_chosen_one/messiah Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) by will of_god, to_the holy ones being and faithful in chosen_one Yēsous: 2 grace to_you_all and peace from god the_father of_us and the_master Yēsous chosen_one.
   3 Blessed be the god and father of_the master of_us, Yēsous chosen_one, the one having_blessed us with every blessing spiritual in the heavenly realms in chosen_one, 4 as he_chose us in him before the_foundation of_the_world, to_be us holy and blameless in_front_of him in love, 5 having_predetermined us for adoption_as_sons through Yēsous chosen_one to himself, according_to the good_pleasure of_the will of_him, 6 to the_praise of_the_glory of_the grace of_him, of_which favoured us in the one having_been_loved, 7 in whom we_are_having redemption through the blood of_him, the forgiveness of_transgressions, according_to the riches of_the grace of_him, 8 of_which he_abounded on us in all wisdom and understanding, 9 having_made_known to_us the mystery of_the will of_him, according_to the good_pleasure of_him, which he_purposed in him, 10 for the_management of_the fullness the of_times, to_sum_up the things all in the chosen_one, the things in the heavens, and the things on the earth, in him, 11 in whom also we_were_allotted_inheritance, having_been_predetermined according_to the_purpose of_the one the things all working, according_to the counsel of_the will of_him, 12 in_order that to_be us, to the_praise of_the_glory of_him, the ones having_previously_hoped in the chosen_one, 13 in whom also you_all having_heard the word of_truth, the gospel of_the salvation of_you_all, in whom also having_believed, you_all_were_sealed with_the spirit of_promise holy, 14 who is the_deposit of_the inheritance of_us, to the_redemption of_the procuring, to the_praise of_the glory of_him.
   15 Because_of this, also_I having_heard the among you_all faith in the master Yēsous, and toward all the holy ones, 16 not I_am_ceasing giving_thanks for you_all, mentioning making in the prayers of_me, 17 in_order_that the god of_the master of_us, Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one/messiah, the father of_glory, may_give to_you_all the_spirit of_wisdom and revelation, in the_knowledge of_him, 18 having_been_enlightened the eyes of_the heart in_order that to_have_known you_all, what is the hope of_the calling of_him, what are the riches of_the glory of_the inheritance of_him, in the holy ones, 19 and what is the surpassing greatness of_the power of_him toward us, the ones believing according_to the working of_the power of_the strength of_him, 20 which he_has_worked in the chosen_one, having_raised him from the_dead, and having_sat_down him at the_right hand of_him, in the heavenly realms, 21 above every rule, and authority, and power, and mastership, and every name being_named, not only in age this, but also in the one coming, 22 and all things subjected under the feet of_him, and of_him gave to_be head concerning all things to_the assembly, 23 which is the body of_him, the fullness of_the one the things all in all filling.
  2  And you_all being dead in_the transgressions and the sins of_you_all, 2 in which once you_all_walked, according_to the age of_world this, according_to the ruler of_the authority of_the air, the spirit now working_in in the sons of_disbelief, 3 among whom also we all were_conducted once in the desires of_the flesh of_us, doing the desires of_the flesh and of_its minds, and we_were children by_nature of_severe_anger, as even the rest.
   4 But god rich being in mercy, because_of the great love of_him, which he_loved us, 5 even being us dead in_our transgressions, he_made_alive_together us with with_the chosen_one (by_grace you_all_are having_been_saved), 6 and he_raised_up_with us and he_sat_together us in the heavenly realms in chosen_one Yēsous, 7 in_order_that he_may_display in the ages, coming_over the surpassing riches of_the grace of_him, in kindness to us in chosen_one Yēsous.
   8 For by_grace you_all_are having_been_saved through faith, also this not of of_you_all, of_god the gift it_is, 9 not from works, in_order_that not anyone may_boast.
   10 For of_him we_are the_workmanship, having_been_created in chosen_one/messiah Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) for works good, which previously_prepared god, in_order_that in them we_may_walk.
   11 Therefore be_remembering that once you_all the pagans in the_flesh, the ones being_called the_uncircumcision by the being_called circumcision in the_flesh handmade, 12 that you_all_were at_time that without chosen_one, having_been_estranged from_the citizenship of_Israēl/(Yisərā'ēl), and strangers to_the covenants of_the promise, hope not having, and godless in the world.
   13 But now in chosen_one Yēsous, you_all who once being far, were_become near by the blood chosen_one.
   14 For he is the peace of_us, the one having_made both one, and the dividing_wall of_the fence having_broken, the enmity in the flesh of_him, 15 the law of_the commandments in decrees having_nullified, in_order_that the two he_may_create in himself into one new man, making peace, 16 and he_may_reconcile both in one body to_god through the cross, having_killed_off the enmity by it, 17 and having_come, he_gospel_preached: peace to_you_all who were far, and peace to_the ones near, 18 because through him we_are_having access, both by one spirit to the father.
   19 Therefore consequently no_longer you_all_are strangers and sojourners, but you_all_are fellow-citizens of_the holy ones and the_household of_god, 20 having_been_built_up on the foundation of_the ambassadors and prophets, being the_cornerstone himself chosen_one Yēsous, 21 in whom the_whole the building being_joined_together, is_growing into a_temple holy in the_master, 22 in whom also you_all are_being_built_together, for a_dwelling_place of_god in the_spirit.
  3  This for_reason_of, I Paulos, the prisoner of_chosen_one Yēsous for you_all pagans, 2 if surely you_all_heard the management of_the grace of_god, having_been_given to_me for you_all, 3 by revelation was_made_known to_me the mystery, as I_previously_wrote in short, 4 to which you_all_are_being_able reading it to_understand the insight of_me in the mystery of_the chosen_one, 5 which in_other generations not was_made_known to_the sons of_men, as now was_revealed to_the holy ambassadors of_him and prophets in the_spirit, 6 to_be the pagans, fellow-heirs, and a_fellow-body_members, and fellow-partakers, of_the promise in chosen_one/messiah Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a), through the gospel, 7 of_which I_was_become a_servant according_to the gift of_the grace of_god having_been_given to_me, according_to the working of_the power of_him.
   8 To_me the, very_least of_all the_holy ones, was_given grace this, to_the pagans to_gospel_preach, the untraceable riches of_the chosen_one, 9 and to_enlighten all, what is the management of_the mystery, having_been_hidden_away from the ages in god, the one the things all having_created, 10 in_order_that may_be_made_known now to_the rulers and the authorities in the heavenly realms through the assembly the manifold wisdom of_god, 11 according_to the_purpose of_the ages, which he_established in the chosen_one Yēsous, the master of_us, 12 in whom we_are_having boldness and access in confidence by the faith in_him.
   13 Therefore I_am_requesting you_all not to_be_losing_heart at the tribulations of_me for you_all, which is the_glory of_you_all.
   14 This for_reason_of, I_am_bowing the knees of_me to the father, 15 of whom every family in the_heavens and on earth is_being_named, 16 in_order_that he_may_give to_you_all according_to the riches of_the glory of_him, with_power to_be_strengthened by the spirit of_him in the inside person, 17 to_dwell_in the chosen_one through faith in the hearts of_you_all in love, having_been_rooted and having_been_established, 18 in_order_that you_all_may_have_strength to_grasp with all the holy ones, what is the breadth, and length, and height, and depth, 19 and to_know the surpassing of_knowledge love of_the chosen_one, in_order_that it_may_be_filled all the fullness of_god.
   20 And to_the one being_able above all things, to_do exceedingly that we_are_requesting or are_understanding, according_to the power working in us, 21 to_him be the glory in the assembly and in chosen_one Yēsous, to all the generations of_the age of_the ages.
   Truly.
   4  Therefore I_am_exhorting you_all, I the prisoner in the_master, worthily to_walk of_the calling to_which you_all_were_called, 2 with all humility and gentleness, with patience, tolerating of_one_another in love, 3 being_earnest to_be_keeping the oneness of_the spirit in the bond of_peace.
   4 ˱There_is˲_one one body and spirit, as also you_all_were_called in one hope of_the calling of_you_all, 5 one one, master one, faith immersion, 6 one god and father of_all, who is over all, and through all, and in all.
   7 But one to_each of_us was_given grace, according_to the measure of_the gift of_the chosen_one/messiah.
   8 Therefore he_is_saying: having_gone_up to height, he_captured captivity, he_gave gifts to_people.
   9 And : he_went_up, what is it, except that also he_came_down into the lower parts of_the earth.
   10 The one having_come_down himself is also the one having_gone_up above all the heavens, in_order_that he_may_fill the things all.
   11 And he gave the on_one_hand ambassadors, on_the_other_hand the prophets, on_the_other_hand the gospel_preachers, on_the_other_hand the shepherds and teachers, 12 with the completing of_the holy ones for the_work of_service, for the_building of_the body of_the chosen_one, 13 until we_may_attain all to the oneness of_the faith, and of_the knowledge of_the son of_god, to a_man mature, to the_measure of_the_stature of_the fullness of_the chosen_one, 14 in_order_that no_longer we_may_be_may_be infants, being_tossed_by_waves and being_carried_about by_every wind of_teaching in the trickery of_people, in craftiness with the scheming of_deception, 15 but being_truthful in love, we_may_grow into him in_the things all, who is the head chosen_one, 16 from whom all the body, being_joined_together and being_held_together by every joint of_its supply according_to the_working in the_measure of_one each part, the growth of_the body is_making to the_building of_itself in love.
   17 Therefore this I_am_saying and I_am_testifying in the_master, no_longer you_all to_be_walking, as also the pagans is_walking in the_uselessness of_the mind of_them.
   18 Having_been_darkened in_their mind being, having_been_estranged from_the life of_god, because_of the ignorance being in them, because_of the hardening of_the heart of_them, 19 who having_languished, themselves gave_over to_wantonness for the_working of_impurity all with greediness.
   20 But you_all not thus learned the chosen_one, 21 if surely him you_all_heard and in him you_all_were_taught, as is the_truth in Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a), 22 to_put_away you_all according_to your earlier conduct the old person, which being_corrupted according_to the desires of_seduction, 23 and to_be_being_renewed in_the spirit of_the mind of_you_all, 24 and dress_in the new person according_to god, having_been_created in righteousness and devoutness of_truth.
   25 Therefore having_put_away falsehood: be_speaking the_truth each with the one near him, because we_are of_one_another members.
   26 Be_being_angered, and_yet not be_sinning, the sun not let_be_set_down on the_anger of_you_all, 27 not be_giving place to_the devil.
   28 The one stealing no_longer let_be_stealing, but rather him _let_be_laboring, working with_his hands good, in_order_that he_may_be_having to_be_sharing to_the one need having.
   29 Any word bad out_of the mouth of_you_all not let_be_going_out, but if any good for building of_the need, in_order_that it_may_give grace to_the ones hearing.
   30 And not be_sorrowing the spirit holy of_god, by whom you_all_were_sealed for the_day of_redemption.
   31 All bitterness, and rage, and severe_anger, and clamor, and slander, let_be_taken_away from you_all with all malice.
   32 Be_becoming to one_another good, tender-hearted, forgiving to_yourselves, as also god in chosen_one/messiah forgave to_you_all.
  5  Therefore be_becoming imitators of_god as children beloved, 2 and be_walking in love, as also the chosen_one loved you_all, and gave_over himself for us, an_offering and a_sacrifice to_god into an_aroma of_a_sweet_fragrance.
   3 But sexual_immorality, and impurity all, or greediness, not it _let_be_being_named among you_all, as is_befitting to_holy ones, 4 and filthiness, and foolish_talk, or crude_joking, which not was_fitting, but rather thanksgiving.
   5 For this be_having/_known knowing that any sexually_immoral, or unclean, or coveter, who is an_idolater, not is_having inheritance in the kingdom of_the chosen_one and of_god.
   6 No_one you_all let_be_seducing with_empty words because_of for, these things is_coming the severe_anger of_god on the sons of_disbelief.
   7 Therefore not be_becoming fellow-partakers with_them, 8 for you_all_were once darkness, but now light in the_master, as children of_light be_walking 9 (for the the_fruit of_the light is in all goodness, and righteousness, and truth), 10 approving what is well_pleasing to_the master.
   11 And not be_participating with_the works unfruitful of_darkness, but rather even be_rebuking them, 12 for the things secretly becoming by them, shameful it_is even to_be_speaking.
   13 But the things all being_rebuked by the light is_being_revealed, for everything being_revealed light is.
   14 Therefore he_is_saying: be_raising, you sleeping, and rise_up from the dead, and will_be_shining_upon ˱upon˲_you the chosen_one.
   15 Therefore be_watching_out exactly how you_all_are_walking, not as unwise, but as wise, 16 redeeming the time, because the days evil are.
   17 Because_of this, not be_becoming foolish, but be_understanding what the will of_the master is.
   18 And not be_being_drunk with_wine, in which is intemperance, but be_being_filled with the_spirit, 19 speaking to_themselves in psalms, and hymns, and songs singing and singing_praises in_the heart of_you_all to_the master, 20 giving_thanks always for all things in the_name of_the master of_us, Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one, to_god even the_father, 21 being_subjected to_one_another in reverence of_chosen_one/messiah.
   22 wives be to_their own husbands as to_the master, 23 because the_husband is head of_the wife, as also the chosen_one is the_head of_the assembly, he the_saviour of_the body.
   24 But as the assembly is_being_subjected to_the chosen_one, thus also the wives to_their husbands in everything.

   4 And, fathers, not be_angering the children of_you_all, but be_nurturing them in the_discipline and admonition of_the_master.
