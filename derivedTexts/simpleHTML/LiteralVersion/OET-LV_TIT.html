Titos

1  Paulos, a_slave of_god, an_ambassador and of_Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one/messiah, according_to the_faith of_the_chosen ones of_god, and knowledge of_the_truth, which is according_to devoutness, 2 in the_hope of_life eternal, which the unlying god promised before times eternal, 3 but revealed the word of_him in_seasons his_own in the_proclamation, which I was_entrusted according_to the_command of_god, the saviour of_us 4 to_Titos, my_genuine child, according_to our_common faith: grace and peace from god the_father and chosen_one Yēsous, the saviour of_us.
5 For_reason_of This, I_left_behind you in Krētē, in_order_that you_may_set_in_order the things lacking, and may_appoint elders in /every/_city as I directed to_you, 6 if anyone is unindictable, the_husband of_one wife, having believing children, not in accusation of_intemperance or insubordinate.
7 For It_is_fitting the overseer to_be unindictable as a_manager of_god, not self-willed, not easily_angered, not winebibbing, not a_bully, not greedy, 8 but hospitable, a_lover_of_good, sensible, righteous, devout, self-controlled, 9 upholding of_the faithful word according_to the teaching, in_order_that he_may_be powerful both to_be_exhorting with teaching being_sound, and to_be_rebuking the ones speaking_against it.
10 For many Are insubordinate, useless_talkers, and deceivers, most_of_all the ones of the circumcision, 11 whom it_is_fitting to_be_silencing, who are_overturning whole households, teaching the things not it_is_fitting, for_reason_of shameful gain.
12 Someone Said of them, an_own prophet of_them: ones_from_Krētē always are liars, evil wild_beasts, idle bellies.
13 This testimony is true.
For which cause be_rebuking them severely, in_order_that they_may_be_being_sound in the faith, 14 not giving_heed to_Youdaiōns myths and the_commandments of_humans turning_away the truth.
15 All things are pure to_the pure, but to_the ones having_been_defiled and unbelievers, nothing is pure, but both the mind, and conscience of_them has_been_defiled.
16 They_are_confessing to_have_known god, but by_their works they_are_disowning him, being detestable and unpersuadable, and unqualified for any good work.
2  You but be_speaking the things it_is_befitting to_being_sound teaching.
2 The_older_men sober to_be, dignified, sensible, being_sound in_faith, in_love, in_endurance.
3 The_older_women likewise in behavior reverent, not diabolical, not to_wine much having_been_enslaved, good_teaching, 4 in_order_that they_are_making_sensible the new women husband-loving to_be, children-loving, 5 sensible, pure, homemakers, good, being_subjected to_their own husbands, in_order_that not the word of_god may_be_being_slandered.
6 The younger men likewise be_exhorting, to_be_being_sensible, 7 about all things yourself bringing_about an_example of_good works, in your teaching, incorruption, dignity, 8 speech healthy, irreproachable, in_order_that the man of contrary may_be_swayed, nothing having to_be_saying concerning us bad.
9 Slaves to_their_own masters to_be_being_subjected in all things, well_pleasing to_be, not speaking_against, 10 not embezzling, but all faith displaying good, in_order_that the teaching of_the saviour of_us, god, they_may_be_adorning in all things.
11 /Has/_appeared for the grace of_god, salvation to_all people, 12 instructing us that having_disowned ungodliness and worldly lusts, sensibly and justly, and devoutly, we_may_live in the present age, 13 waiting_for the blessed hope, and the_appearing of_the glory of_the great god and saviour of_us, Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a), chosen_one/messiah 14 who gave himself for us, in_order_that he_may_redeem us from all lawlessness, and may_purify to_himself a_people chosen, zealous of_good works.
15 These things be_speaking, and be_exhorting, and be_rebuking, with all command.
No_one of_you let_be_disregarding.
3  Be_reminding them to_rulers, to_authorities, to_be_being_subjected, to_be_yielding, for every work good ready to_be, 2 no_one to_be_slandering, peaceable to_be, gentle, all displaying gentleness toward all people.
3 Were for once also we foolish, unpersuadable, being_strayed, serving in_lusts and gratifications various, in malice and envy going_by, hateful, hating one_another.
4 When but the kindness and the love_of_mankind has_appeared of_the saviour of_us, god, 5 not by works in righteousness, that practiced we, but according_to the of_him mercy, he_saved us, through the_washing of_regeneration and renewal of_the_spirit holy, 6 whom he_poured_out on us richly, through Yēsous chosen_one, the saviour of_us, 7 in_order_that having_been_justified by_the of_that one grace, heirs we_may_be_become, according_to the_hope of_life eternal.
8 Trustworthy is the saying, and concerning these things I_am_wishing you to_be_confirming: in_order_that may_be_caring of_good works to_be_leading, the ones having_believed in_god.
These things is good and beneficial to_people.
9 Foolish but debates, and genealogies, and strifes, and quarrels legal, be_avoiding, they_are for unprofitable and useless.
10 A_sectarian person after one and a_second admonition be_refusing, 11 having_known that has_been_perverted such person and is_sinning, being self-condemned.
12 Whenever I_may_send Artemas to you or Tuⱪikos, be_earnest to_come to me into Nikopolis, there for I_have_judged to_winter.
13 Zēnas the lawyer and Apollōs earnestly send_forward, in_order_that nothing to_them may_be_lacking.
14 Let_be_learning and also our people of_good works to_be_leading for the necessary needs, in_order_that not they_may_be unfruitful.
15 Are_greeting you the ones with me all.
Greet the ones loving us in the_faith.
grace be with all of_you_all.
