3 Yōannēs

1  The elder, to_Gaios the beloved, whom I am_loving in truth.
2 Beloved, concerning all things I_am_hoping you to_be_being_prospered and to_be_being_healthy, as the soul of_you is_being_prospered.
3 For I_was_ exceedingly _elated the_brothers coming and testifying of_you in_the truth, as you are_walking in the_truth.
4 I_am_ not _having joy greater than these things, that I_may_be_hearing my children walking in the truth.
5 Beloved, you_are_doing faithful whatever, you_may_do for the brothers and this is for_strangers, 6 who testified before the_assembly to_the love of_you, whom you_will_be_doing well, having_sent_forward worthily of_god.
7 For they_came_out for the name, receiving nothing from the pagans.
8 Therefore we ought to_be_supporting such men, in_order_that we_may_be_becoming fellow-workers with_the truth.
9 I_wrote something to_the assembly, but the one loving_to_be_first of_them, Diotrefēs, is_ not _accepting us.
10 Because_of this, if I_may_come, I_will_be_reminding the works of_him, which he_is_doing, rambling_against us with_ evil _words, and not being_sufficed with these things, nor himself is_accepting the brothers, but he_is_forbidding the ones wishing, and is_throwing_ them _out from the assembly.
11 Beloved, be_imitating not evil, but good.
The one doing_good is of god, the one doing_evil has_ not _seen god.
12 To_Dēmētrios has_been_attested by all, and by the truth itself, and we also are_testifying, and you_have_known that the testimony of_us is true.
13 I_was_having many things to_write to_you, but I_am_ not _wanting to_be_writing to_you through ink and pen, 14 but I_am_hoping to_see you immediately, and we_will_be_speaking mouth before mouth.
15 Peace be to_you.
The friends are_greeting you.
Be_greeting the friends by name.
