   2 We_are_giving_thanks to_god always concerning all of_you_all, remembrance making in the prayers of_us, unceasingly 3 remembering of_you_all about_the work of_faith, and the labor of_love, and the endurance of_hope of_the master of_us, G‹H‹Yēsous chosen_one, before the god and father of_us, 4 having_known, brothers having_been_loved by god, the choice of_you_all, 5 because the gospel of_us not was_become to you_all in word only, but also in power, and in the_spirit holy, and full_assurance much, as you_all_have_known such_as we_were_become you_all because_of you_all.
   6 And you_all imitators of_us were_become and of_the master, having_received the word in tribulation much with the_joy of_the_spirit holy, 7 so_as to_become you_all examples to_all the ones believing in G‹Makedonia›G and in G‹Aⱪaia›G.

   9 For they concerning us are_reporting what_kind entrance we_had to you_all, and how you_all_turned_back to god from idols, to_be_serving for_the_god living and true, 10 and to_be_waiting for_the son of_him from the heavens, whom he_raised from the dead, G‹H‹Yēsous, the one rescuing us from the severe_anger coming.
  2  For yourselves you_all_have_known, brothers, the entrance of_us to you_all, that not vain it_has_become, 2 but having_previously_suffered and having_been_mistreated, as you_all_have_known, in Rx-G‹Filippou›G-Rx we_spoke_boldly in the god of_us, to_speak to you_all the gospel of_god among much struggle.
   3 For the exhortation of_us not of deception, nor of impurity, nor in deceit, 4 but as we_have_been_approved by god, to_be_entrusted the gospel, thus we_are_speaking, not as to_people bringing_pleasure, but to_god, the one approving the hearts of_us.
   5 For neither once with word of_flattery we_were_become, as you_all_have_known, nor with a_under_pretense for_greediness (god is witness) 6 nor seeking from people glory, neither from you_all, nor from others, 7 being_able in burden to_be, as of_chosen_one/messiah ambassadors.
   But we_were_become infants in the_midst of_you_all, as a_nursing_mother may_be_cherishing the of_herself children.
   8 Thus longing for_you_all, we_were_taking_pleasure to_share with_you_all, not only the gospel of_god, but also the of_ourselves lives, because beloved to_us you_all_were_become.
   9 For you_all_are_remembering, brothers, the labor of_us and hardship, night and day working, in_order that not to_burden anyone of_you_all, we_proclaimed to you_all the gospel of_god.
   10 You_all are witnesses and god, how devoutly, and justly, and blamelessly, to_you_all the ones believing we_were_become, 11 just_as you_all_have_known how one each of_you_all, as a_father to_children of_himself, 12 exhorting you_all, and consoling, and testifying, in_order that to_be_walking you_all worthily of_god, the one having_called you_all into the of_himself kingdom and glory.
   13 And because_of this also we are_giving_thanks to_god unceasingly that having_received the_word by_hearing from us of_god, you_all_received not the_word of_humans, but as truly it_is, the_word of_god, which also is_working in you_all believing.
   14 For you_all imitators were_become, brothers, of_the assemblies of_god, being in Rx-G‹Youdaia›G-Rx in chosen_one G‹H‹Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a)›H›G, because the same things you_all_suffered, also you_all by your own fellow-countrymen, as also they did by the G‹Youdaiōns›G, 15 who both the master having_killed_off G‹H‹Yēsous, and their prophets, and us having_driven_out, and to_god not bringing_pleasure, but to_all people of_contrary, 16 forbidding us to_the pagans to_speak, in_order_that they_may_be_saved, for which to_fulfill of_them the sins always.
   But came on them the severe_anger to end.
   17 But we, brothers, having_been_orphaned of you_all, for the_time of_an_hour, in_face not in_heart, more_abundantly we_were_earnest the face of_you_all to_see with great desire.
   18 Because we_wanted to_come to you_all, I indeed G‹Paulos›G, both once and twice, and hindered us G‹H‹Satan/(Sāţān)›H›G.
   19 For who is of_us the_hope or the_joy or the_crown of_boasting.
   Or not are even you_all, before the master of_us, G‹H‹Yēsous, at the of_him coming.
   20 For you_all are the glory of_us, and the_joy.
  3  Therefore no_longer enduring, we_took_pleasure to_be_left in G‹Athēnai›G only, 2 and we_sent G‹Timotheos›G, the brother of_us, and servant of_god in the gospel of_the chosen_one/messiah, in_order that to_strengthen you_all and to_exhort, concerning the faith of_you_all, 3 that no_one to_be_being_disturbed by tribulations these.
   For yourselves you_all_have_known that for this we_are_appointing.
   4 For also when with you_all we_were, we_were_previously_saying to_you_all that we_are_going to_be_being_oppressed, as also it_became and you_all_have_known.
   5 Because_of this also_I no_longer enduring, I_sent in_order that to_know the faith of_you_all, lest somehow tempted you_all the one tempting, and in vain may_become the labor of_us.
   6 But now having_come G‹Timotheos›G to us from you_all, and having_gospel_preached to_us about_the faith and the love of_you_all, and that you_all_are_having a_mentioning of_us good always, longing us to_see, just_as also we you_all.
   7 Because_of this we_were_comforted, brothers, by you_all in all the distress and tribulation of_us, through the of_you_all faith.
   8 Because now we_are_living, if you_all are_standing_firm in the_master.
   9 For what thanksgiving we_are_being_able to_the to_god to_repay concerning you_all, for all the joy that we_are_rejoicing because_of you_all, before the god of_us.
   10 Night and day, beyond_exceedingly beseeching in_order that to_see of_you_all the face, and to_prepare the lacking things in_the_faith of_you_all.
   11 And himself the god and father of_us, and the master of_us, G‹H‹Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a)›H›G, might_direct the way of_us to you_all.
   12 And you_all the master might_abound and might_abound in_love toward one_another, and toward all, just_as also we toward you_all, 13 in_order that to_strengthen of_you_all the hearts, blameless in holiness before the god and father of_us, at the coming of_the master of_us, G‹H‹Yēsous, with all the holy ones of_him.
   Truly.
   4  Therefore finally, brothers, we_are_asking you_all and we_are_exhorting in the the_master G‹H‹Yēsous, as you_all_received from us, how it_is_fitting you_all to_be_walking and to_be_bringing_pleasure to_god (as even you_all_are_walking), in_order_that you_all_may_be_abounding more.
   2 For you_all_have_known what_all charges we_gave to_you_all through the master G‹H‹Yēsous.

   7 For not called us god to impurity, but in holiness.
   8 So_then the one rejecting this, not a_human is_rejecting, but god, the one giving the spirit of_him holy to you_all.
   9 And concerning brotherly_love, no need you_all_are_having for_me to_be_writing to_you_all, for yourselves you_all god-taught are, in_order that to_be_loving one_another.
   10 For also you_all_are_doing it toward all the brothers, who are in all G‹Makedonia›G.
   But we_are_exhorting you_all, brothers, to_be_abounding more, 11 and to_be_being_ambitious to_be_living_quietly and to_be_doing your own things, and to_be_working with_your own hands of_you_all, as to_you_all we_commanded, 12 in_order_that you_all_may_be_walking properly toward the ones outside, and of_nothing need you_all_may_be_having.
   13 Not but we_are_wanting you_all to_be_not_knowing, brothers, concerning the ones being_fallen_asleep, in_order_that not you_all _may_be_being_sorrowed, as also the rest, the ones not having hope.
   14 For if we_are_believing that G‹H‹Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a)›H›G died_off and rose_up, thus also god the ones having_been_fallen_asleep through G‹H‹Yēsous will_be_bringing with him.
   15 For this to_you_all we_are_saying in the_word of_the_master, that we the ones living, being_remaining to the coming of_the master, by_no_means not may_precede the ones having_been_fallen_asleep.
   16 Because himself the master with a_loud_command, with the_voice of_an_archangel, and with the_trumpet of_god, will_be_coming_down from heaven, and the dead in chosen_one/messiah will_be_rising_up first, 17 then we the ones living, being_remaining together with them, will_be_being_snatched in the_clouds for the_meeting of_the master in the_air, and thus always with the_master we_will_be.
   18 So_then be_comforting one_another with words these.
  5  And concerning the times and the seasons, brothers, no need you_all_are_having to_you_all to_be_being_written.
   2 For yourselves exactly you_all_have_known that the_day of_the_master, as a_thief by night thus is_coming.
   3 Whenever they_may_be_saying: Peace and security, then sudden is_recognizing to_them destruction, as the birth_pains to_the woman in pregnant having a_child, and by_no_means not they_may_escape.
   4 But you_all, brothers, not are in darkness, in_order_that the day you_all like thieves may_grasp.
   5 For all you_all sons of_light are, and sons of_day.
   Neither we_are of_night nor of_darkness.
   6 Therefore consequently not we_may_be_sleeping as the rest, but we_may_be_watching and we_may_be_being_sober.
   7 For the ones sleeping, by_night are_sleeping, and the ones being_drunk, by_night are_being_drunk.
   8 But we of_the_day being, may_be_being_sober, having_dressed_in the_breastplate of_faith and love, and the_helmet, the_hope of_salvation.

   15 Be_seeing not someone evil for evil to_anyone may_give_back, but always the good be_pursuing, and toward one_another and toward all.
   16 Always be_rejoicing.
   17 Unceasingly be_praying.
   18 In everything be_giving_thanks, for this is the_will of_god in chosen_one/messiah G‹H‹Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a)›H›G toward you_all.
   19 The spirit not be_extinguishing.
   20 Prophesies not be_scorning.

   23 And himself god of_peace might_sanctify you_all complete, and complete of_you_all the spirit, and soul, and body, blamelessly at the coming of_the master of_us, G‹H‹Yēsous chosen_one, might_have_kept.
   24 Faithful is the one calling you_all, who also will_be_performing it.
   25 Brothers, be_praying also for us.
