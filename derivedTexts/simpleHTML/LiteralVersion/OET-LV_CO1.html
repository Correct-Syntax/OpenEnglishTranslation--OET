1 Korinthios

1  Paulos, a_called ambassador of_chosen_one/messiah Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) by the_will of_god, and Sōsthenēs, our brother, 2 to_the assembly of_god, having_been_sanctified in chosen_one Yēsous, being in Korinthos, called holy ones, with all the ones calling on_the name of_the master of_us, Yēsous chosen_one, in every place, of_them and of_us: 3 grace to_you_all and peace from god the_father of_us and the_master Yēsous chosen_one.
   4 I_am_giving_thanks to_the god of_me always concerning you_all, for the grace of_god having_been_given to_you_all in chosen_one Yēsous, 5 that in everything you_all_were_made_rich in him, in all speech and all knowledge, 6 as the testimony of_the chosen_one was_confirmed in you_all, 7 so_as you_all not to_be_being_lacked in nothing a_gift, eagerly_waiting the revelation of_the master of_us, Yēsous chosen_one, 8 who also will_be_confirming you_all to the_end, unindictable in the day of_the master of_us, Yēsous 9 Faithful is god, by whom you_all_were_called into fellowship of_the son of_him, Yēsous chosen_one, the master of_us.
   10 And I_am_exhorting you_all, brothers, by the name of_the master of_us, Yēsous chosen_one, in_order_that the same thing you_all_may_be_speaking all, and not may_be among you_all divisions, but you_all_may_be having_been_prepared in the same mind and in the same opinion.
   11 For it_was_made_evident to_me concerning you_all, brothers by the ones of_Ⱪloē, that quarrels among you_all are.
   12 And I_am_saying this, that each of_you_all is_saying: I on_one_hand am of_Paulos: On_the_other_hand I am of_Apollōs: On_the_other_hand I am of_Kēfas: On_the_other_hand I am of_chosen_one.
   13 /Has_been_divided the chosen_one.
   Not Paulos was_impaled_on_a_stake for you_all, or into the name of_Paulos you_all_were_immersed.
   14 I_am_giving_thanks that no_one of_you_all I_immersed, except Krispos and Gaios, 15 in_order_that not anyone may_say that into my name you_all_were_immersed.
   16 And I_immersed also the of_Stefanas household, furthermore not I_have_known whether any other I_immersed.
   17 For not sent_out me chosen_one/messiah to_be_immersing, but to_be_gospel_preaching, not in wisdom of_word, in_order_that not may_be_voided the cross of_the chosen_one.
   18 The for word of_the cross, to_the ones on_one_hand perishing foolishness it_is, on_the_other_hand to_the ones being_saved to_us the_power of_god it_is.
   19 For it_has_been_written: I_will_be_destroying the wisdom of_the wise, and the intelligence of_the intelligent I_will_be_rejecting.
   20 Where is the_wise.
   Where is the_scribe.
   Where is the_debater of_age this.
   Not made_foolish god the wisdom of_the world.
   21 For since in the wisdom of_god, not knew the world by its wisdom god, took_pleasure god through the foolishness of_the proclamation to_save the ones believing.
   22 Since both the_Youdaiōns signs are_requesting, and the_Hellēns wisdom are_seeking, 23 but we are_proclaiming chosen_one having_been_impaled_on_a_stake, to_the_Youdaiōns on_one_hand a_offense, on_the_other_hand to_pagans foolishness, 24 but to_them the called ones, to_the_Youdaiōns both and to_the_Hellēns, chosen_one of_god the_power, and of_god the_wisdom.
   25 Because the foolish of_god, wiser than humans is, and the weak of_god, stronger than humans.
   26 For you_all_are_seeing the calling of_you_all, brothers, that not many wise according_to the_flesh, not many powerful, not many noble.
   27 But the foolish things of_the world chose god, in_order_that he_may_be_disgracing the wise, and the weak things of_the world chose god, in_order_that he_may_be_disgracing the strong, 28 and the insignificant of_the world and the things having_been_scorned chose god, the things not being, in_order_that the things being he_may_nullify, 29 so_that not may_boast all flesh before god.
   30 Out_of but him, you_all are in chosen_one Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a), who was_become wisdom to_us from god, righteousness jointly, and holiness, and redemption, 31 in_order_that as it_has_been_written: the one boasting, in the_master him _let_be_boasting.
  2  And_I having_come to you_all, brothers, came not according_to prominence of_speech or wisdom, proclaiming to_you_all the mystery of_god.
   2 For not I_judged anything to_have_known among you_all, except Yēsous chosen_one/messiah, and this one having_been_impaled_on_a_stake.
   3 And_I in weakness, and in fear, and in trembling much became with you_all.
   4 And the word of_me and the proclamation of_me, not in persuasive of_wisdom words, but in demonstration of_the_spirit and of_power, 5 in_order_that the faith of_you_all, not may_be in wisdom of_humans, but in the_power of_god.
   6 And wisdom we_are_speaking among the mature, but wisdom, not of_age this, nor of_the the_rulers of_age this, the ones being_nullified, 7 but we_are_speaking of_god the_wisdom in a_mystery having_been_hidden_away, which predetermined god before the ages, for the_glory of_us, 8 which no_one of_the rulers of_age this has_known, for if they_knew, not would the master of_glory they_impaled_on_a_stake, 9 but as it_has_been_written: what eye not saw, and ear not heard, and in the_heart of_a_person not went_up, which prepared god for_the ones loving him.
   10 To_us for revealed it god through the spirit, for the spirit all things is_searching, even the depths of_god.
   11 For who has_known ˱among˲_people the things of_the person, except the spirit of_the person which is in him.
   Thus also the things of_god no_one has_known, except the spirit of_god.
   12 And we not the spirit of_the world received, but the spirit who is from god, in_order_that we_may_have_known the things by god having_been_granted to_us.
   13 Which also we_are_speaking, not in taught of_human wisdom words, but in those taught of_the_spirit, by_spiritual things spiritual things communicating.
   14 But the_soulish person, not is_receiving the things of_the spirit of_god, for foolishness to_him it_is, and not he_is_able to_know them, because spiritually it_is_being_examined.
   15 But the spiritual one is_examining the things all, but he by no_one is_being_examined.
   16 For who knew the_mind of_the_master, who will_be_teaching him.
   But we the_mind of_chosen_one are_having.

   But neither now you_all_are_being_able, 3 for still fleshly you_all_are.
   For where among you_all is jealousy and strife, not fleshly you_all_are and according_to human_origin you_all_are_walking.
   4 For whenever may_be_saying someone: I on_one_hand am of_Paulos, on_the_other_hand another I am of_Apollōs, not humans you_all_are.
   5 Therefore what is Apollōs.
   And what is Paulos.
   Servants through whom you_all_believed, also to_each as the master gave.
   6 I planted, Apollōs gave_to_drink, but god was_growing.
   7 So_that neither the one planting is anything, nor the one giving_to_drink, but the one growing, god.
   8 The one and planting, and the one giving_to_drink, one are, but each his own reward will_be_receiving, according_to his own labor.
   9 For of_god we_are fellow-workers, of_god the_field, of_god the_building you_all_are.
   10 According_to the grace of_god having_been_given to_me, as a_wise architect the_foundation I_laid, but another is_building_on it.
   But each let_be_seeing, how he_is_building_on.
   11 For foundation another no_one is_able to_lay, beside the one lying, which is Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one.
   12 And if anyone is_building_on on the foundation gold, and silver, stones precious, wood, grass, straw, 13 of_each the work manifest will_be_becoming, for the day will_be_making_evident it, because in fire it_is_being_revealed, and of_each his work, what_kind it_is, the fire itself will_be_approving.
   14 If of_anyone the work will_be_remaining, which he_built_up, a_reward he_will_be_receiving.
   15 If of_anyone the work will_be_being_burned_up, he_will_be_being_lost, but he will_be_being_saved, but thus as through the_fire.
   16 Not you_all_have_known that temple of_god you_all_are, and the spirit of_god is_dwelling in you_all.
   17 If anyone the temple of_god is_destroying, will_be_destroying this one god, for the temple of_god holy is, which are you_all.
   18 No_one himself let_be_deceiving, if anyone is_supposing wise to_be among you_all in age this, foolish him_let_become, in_order_that he_may_become wise.
   19 For the wisdom of_world this, foolishness with god is.
   For it_has_been_written: the one catching the wise in the craftiness of_them he_is.
   20 And again: the_master is_knowing the speculations of_the wise, that they_are useless.
   21 So_then no_one let_be_boasting in people, for all things of_you_all is, 22 whether Paulos, or Apollōs, or Kēfas, or the_world, or life, or death, or having_presented, or coming, all things of_you_all are, 23 and you_all are of_chosen_one/messiah, And chosen_one is of_god.
  4  Thus us let_be_reckoning a_person as attendants of_chosen_one, and managers of_the_mysteries of_god.
   2 Here furthermore it_is_being_required among the managers that faithful anyone may_be_found.
   3 But to_me to the_least it_is, in_order_that by you_all I_may_be_examined, or by a_human day, but neither myself I_am_examining.
   4 For nothing to_myself I_have_become_aware_of, but not by this I_have_been_justified, but the one examining me the_master is.
   5 So_that not before the_time anything be_judging, until may_come the master, who both will_be_illuminating the hidden things of_the_darkness, and will_be_revealing the counsels of_the hearts, and then the praise will_be_becoming to_each from god.
   6 But these things, brothers, I_adapted to myself and Apollōs because_of you_all, in_order_that in us you_all_may_learn, not beyond what has_been_written, in_order_that not one above one, you_all _may_be_being_arrogant against the other.
   7 For who you is_distinguishing.
   And what you_are_having which not you_received.
   And if also you_received, why you_are_boasting as not having_received.
   8 Already having_been_satiated you_all_are, already you_all_became_rich, apart_from us you_all_reigned, and I_wish surely you_all_reigned, in_order_that also we with_you_all may_reign_with.
   9 For I_am_supposing god us ambassadors last demonstrated, as sentenced_to_death, because a_theatre we_were_become to_the world, both to_angels and to_humans.
   10 We are foolish because_of chosen_one, but you_all prudent in chosen_one, we are weak, but you_all are strong, you_all are glorious, but we dishonorable.
   11 Until the now hour, both we_are_hungering and we_are_thirsting, and we_are_poorly_clothed, and we_are_being_beaten, and we_are_wandering, 12 and we_are_laboring, working with_our own hands.
   Being_insulted, we_are_blessing, being_persecuted, we_are_tolerating, 13 being_defamed, we_are_imploring, as the_scum of_the world we_were_become, of_all refuse until now.
   14 Not swaying you_all I_am_writing these things, but as children of_me beloved, I_am_admonishing.
   15 For if ten_thousand tutors you_all_may_be_having in chosen_one/messiah, but not many fathers, for in chosen_one Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) through the gospel, I you_all bore.
   16 Therefore I_am_exhorting you_all, imitators of_me be_becoming.
   17 Because_of this I_sent to_you_all Timotheos, who is of_me the_child, beloved and faithful in the_master, who you_all will_be_reminding of_the ways of_me in chosen_one Yēsous, as everywhere in every assembly I_am_teaching.
   18 When not and coming of_me to you_all, were_arrogant some.
   19 But I_will_be_coming quickly to you_all, if the master may_will, and I_will_be_knowing, not the speech of_the ones having_been_arrogant, but their power.
   20 For not in word the kingdom of_god is, but in power.
   21 What you_all_are_wanting.
   With a_rod I_may_come to you_all, or in love, and a_spirit of_gentleness.

   4 In the name of_the master of_us, Yēsous having_been_gathered_together you_all and of_mine in_spirit, with the power of_the master of_us Yēsous, 5 to_give_over such one to_Satan/(Sāţān) for the_destruction of_his flesh, in_order_that his spirit may_be_saved in the day of_the of_master.

   35 And if anything to_learn they_are_wanting, at home their own husbands them _let_be_asking, for shameful for_a_woman to_be_speaking in the_assembly.
   36 Either from you_all the word of_god came_out, or to you_all only it_arrived.
   37 If anyone is_supposing a_prophet to_be or spiritual, let_be_knowing what I_am_writing to_you_all that of_the_master it_is a_commandment.
   38 But if anyone is_not_knowing, him _let_be_not_knowing.
   39 So_as, brothers, be_being_zealous to_be_prophesying, and to_be_speaking not be_forbidding with with_tongues.
   40 But all things properly and with order let_be_becoming.
  15  And I_am_making_known to_you_all, brothers, the gospel that I_gospel_preached to_you_all, which also you_all_received, in which also you_all_have_stood, 2 by which also you_all_are_being_saved, to_what word I_gospel_preached to_you_all, if you_all_are_keeping, besides except vainly you_all_believed.
   3 For I_gave_over to_you_all in the_first place, what also I_received: that chosen_one/messiah died_off for the sins of_us according_to the scriptures, 4 and that he_was_buried, and that he_has_been_raised on_the day third according_to the scriptures, 5 and that he_was_seen by_Kēfas, thereafter to_the twelve.
   6 Then he_was_seen by_over five_hundred brothers all_at_once, of whom the greater are_remaining until now, but some were_fallen_asleep.
   7 Then he_was_seen by_Yakōbos/(Ya'aqov), then to_the ambassadors all, 8 and lastly of_all, as_though to_the miscarriage, he_was_seen by_also_me.
   9 For I am the least of_the ambassadors, who not am worthy to_be_being_called an_ambassador, because I_persecuted the assembly of_god.
   10 But by_the_grace of_god, I_am what I_am, and the grace of_him toward me, not vain was_become, but more_abundantly than them all I_labored, not and I, but the grace of_god with me.
   11 Therefore whether I or those, thus we_are_proclaiming, and thus you_all_believed.
   12 And if chosen_one is_being_proclaimed, that from the_dead he_has_been_raised, how are_saying among you_all some that a_resurrection of_the_dead not is.
   13 But if a_resurrection of_the_dead not is, neither chosen_one has_been_raised, 14 and if chosen_one not has_been_raised, vain consequently the proclamation of_us is, vain also the faith of_you_all is.
   15 And we_are_being_found also false_witnesses of_god, because we_testified concerning god that he_raised the chosen_one, whom not he_raised, if_indeed consequently the_dead not are_being_raised.
   16 For if the_dead not are_being_raised, neither chosen_one has_been_raised, 17 and if chosen_one not has_been_raised, useless the faith of_you_all, and still you_all_are in the sins of_you_all.
   18 Consequently also the ones having_been_fallen_asleep in chosen_one perished.
   19 If in life this, in chosen_one having_hoped we_are only, more_pitiful than all people we_are.
   20 But now chosen_one has_been_raised from the_dead, first-fruit of_the ones having_been_fallen_asleep.
   21 For since by a_man death is, also by a_man resurrection of_the_dead is.
   22 For as in Adam/('Ādām), all are_dying_off, thus also in the chosen_one, all will_be_being_given_life.
   23 But each in his own order: the_first-fruit chosen_one/messiah, then the ones of_the chosen_one at the coming of_him, 24 thereafter the end, whenever he_may_be_giving_over the kingdom to_god even the_father, whenever he_may_nullify all beginning, and all authority, and power.
   25 For it_is_fitting him to_be_reigning, until of_which he_may_put all his enemies under the feet of_him.
   26 The_last enemy is_being_nullified is death.
   27 For all things he_subjected under the feet of_him.
   But whenever he_may_say all things has_been_subjected, evident it_is that besides the one having_subjected to_him the things all.
   28 And whenever may_be_subjected to_him the things all, then also himself the son, will_be_being_subjected to_the one having_subjected to_him the things all, in_order_that may_be god all things in all things.
   29 Otherwise what they_will_be_doing, who are being_immersed for the dead.
   If actually the_dead not are_being_raised, why also they_are_being_immersed for them.
   30 Why also we are_risking_danger every hour.
   31 In_every day I_am_dying_off, by your boasting, brothers, which I_am_having in chosen_one Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a), the master of_us.
   32 If according_to human_origin, I_fought_wild_beasts in Efesos, what to_me the profit.
   If the_dead not are_being_raised: we_may_eat and we_may_drink, for tomorrow we_are_dying_off.
   33 Not be_being_deceived, are_corrupting character good conversations evil.
   34 Sober_up justly and not be_sinning, for ignorance of_god some are_having, to shame to_you_all I_am_speaking.
   35 But will_be_saying someone: How are_being_raised the dead.
   And with_what body they_are_coming.
   36 Foolish.
   You what you_are_sowing, not is_being_given_life, if not it_may_die_off.
   37 And what you_are_sowing, not the body going_to_become you_are_sowing, but a_naked seed, if might_happen of_wheat, or of_something of_the rest.
   38 But god is_giving to_it a_body, as he_willed, and to_each of_the seeds, its_own body.
   39 Not all flesh is the same flesh, but another on_one_hand of_humans, on_the_other_hand another flesh of_beasts, on_the_other_hand another flesh of_birds, on_the_other_hand another of_fishes.
   40 And there_are bodies heavenly, and bodies earthly.
   But another on_one_hand the of_the heavenly glory, on_the_other_hand another the of_the earthly.
   41 Another glory of_the_sun, and another glory of_the_moon, and another glory of_the_stars, for star by_star is_carrying_value in glory.
   42 Thus also the resurrection of_the dead.
   It_is_being_sown in corruption, it_is_being_raised in indestructibility, 43 it_is_being_sown in dishonor, it_is_being_raised in glory, it_is_being_sown in weakness, it_is_being_raised in power, 44 it_is_being_sown a_body natural, it_is_being_raised a_body spiritual.
   If there_is a_body natural, there_is also a_spiritual body.
   45 Thus also it_has_been_written: became the first man, Adam/('Ādām): into a_soul living.
   The last Adam into a_spirit giving_life.
   46 But not first the spiritual, but the natural, then the spiritual.
   47 The first man out_of earth is, earthy, the second man is, from heaven.
   48 Such_as the earthy one, such also the earthy ones, and such_as the heavenly one, such also the heavenly ones.
   49 And as we_bore the image of_the of_the_earthy one, we_may_bear also the image of_the heavenly one.
   50 And this I_am_saying, brothers, that flesh and blood the_kingdom of_god to_inherit not is_able, nor corruption, indestructibility is_inheriting.
   51 See a_mystery to_you_all I_am_telling: all not we_will_be_being_fallen_asleep, not, but all we_will_be_being_changed, 52 in an_instant, in the_twinkling of_an_eye, at the last trumpet, for he_will_be_trumpeting, and the dead will_be_being_raised indestructible, and we we_will_be_being_changed.
   53 For it_is_fitting perishable this to_dress_in indestructibility, and mortal this to_dress_in immortality.
   54 But whenever perishable this may_dress_in indestructibility, and mortal this may_dress_in immortality, then will_be_becoming the word having_been_written: was_swallowed_up death in victory.
   55 Where is of_you, death, the victory.
   Where is of_you, death, the sting.
   56 And the the_sting of_death is sin, and the the_power of_sin is the law.
   57 But to_god thankfulness be, to_the one giving us the victory through the master of_us, Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one/messiah.
   58 So_then, brothers of_me beloved, firm be_becoming, immovable, abounding in the work of_the master always, having_known that the labor of_you_all not is vain in the_master.
  16  And concerning the collection which is for the holy ones, as I_directed to_the assemblies of_Galatia, thus also you_all do.
   2 In_every first day of_the_week, each of_you_all by himself let_be_putting, storing_up whatever anything may_be_being_prospered, in_order_that not whenever I_may_come, then collections may_be_becoming.
   3 And whenever I_may_arrive, whomever you_all_may_approve, by letters these I_will_be_sending, to_carry_away the grace of_you_all to Hierousalēm/(Yərūshālam).
   4 And if worthy it_may_be also_I to_be_going, with me they_will_be_going.
   5 But I_will_be_coming to you_all, whenever Makedonia I_may_pass_through, For Makedonia I_am_passing_through.
   6 With and you_all having_happened I_will_be_continuing_with, or I_will_be_wintering, in_order_that you_all me may_send_forward, where if I_may_be_going.
   7 Not for I_am_wanting you_all now in passage to_see, I_am_hoping for a_time certain to_remain_on with you_all, if the master may_permit.
   8 But I_will_be_remaining_on in Efesos until pentecost, 9 for a_door to_me has_opened_up great and active, and opposing many are.
   10 And if may_come Timotheos, be_seeing that fearlessly he_may_become with you_all, for the the_work of_the_master he_is_doing, as I.
   11 Not therefore anyone him may_scorn.
   But send_forward him in peace, in_order_that he_may_come to me, for I_am_awaiting him with the brothers.
   12 But concerning Apollōs the brother, much I_am_exhorting him that he_may_come to you_all with the brothers, and certainly not it_was his_will that now he_may_come, but he_will_be_coming whenever he_may_have_opportunity.
   13 Be_watching, be_standing_firm in the faith, be_masculine, be_being_strengthened.
   14 All things of_you_all in love let_be_becoming.
   15 And I_am_exhorting you_all, brothers, you_all_have_known the house of_Stefanas that it_is the_first-fruit of_Aⱪaia, and to the_service to_the holy ones they_determined themselves, 16 that also you_all may_be_being_subjected to_such men, and to_everyone working_together and laboring.
   17 But I_am_rejoicing at the coming of_Stefanas, and Fortounatos, and Aⱪaikos, because of_you_all the_lacking these fulfilled.
   18 For they_refreshed my spirit and of_you_all.
   Therefore be_recognizing such men.
   19 Are_greeting you_all the assemblies of_Asia.
   Are_greeting you_all in the_master much Akulas and Priska, with the in house of_them assembly.
   20 Are_greeting you_all the brothers all.
   Greet one_another with a_kiss holy.
   21 The greeting is of_my /own/_hand, of_Paulos.
   22 If anyone not is_loving the master, him_let_be a_curse.
   Marana qa.
   23 The grace of_the master Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one/messiah with you_all be.
   24 The love of_me be with all of_you_all in chosen_one Yēsous.
   Truly.
