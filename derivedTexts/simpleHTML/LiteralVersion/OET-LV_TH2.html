2 G‹ones_from_Thessalonikē›G

1  G‹Paulos›G, and G‹Silouanos›G, and G‹Timotheos›G, to_the assembly of_the_G‹ones_from_Thessalonikē›G, in god the_father of_us and the_master G‹H‹Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a)›H›G chosen_one/messiah: 2 grace be to_you_all and peace from god the_father of_us and the_master G‹H‹Yēsous chosen_one.
   3 To_be_giving_thanks we_ought to_god always concerning you_all, brothers, as worthy it_is, because is_growing_beyond the faith of_you_all, and is_abounding the love of_one each, of_all of_you_all, to one_another, 4 so_as ourselves us in you_all to_be_boasting, in the assemblies of_god, for the endurance of_you_all, and faith in all the persecutions of_you_all, and in_the tribulations that you_all_are_tolerating, 5 a_display of_the righteous judgment of_god, in_order that to_be_considered_worthy you_all of_the kingdom of_god, for which also you_all_are_suffering, 6 if_indeed righteous it_is with god, to_repay to_the ones oppressing you_all in_tribulation, 7 and to_you_all being_oppressed, relaxation with us at the revelation of_the master G‹H‹Yēsous from heaven, with angels of_power of_him, 8 in a_fire of_flame giving vengeance on_the ones not having_known god, and on_the ones not submitting to_the gospel of_the master of_us, G‹H‹Yēsous, chosen_one, 9 who justice will_be_paying with_destruction eternal, from the_presence of_the master, and from the glory of_the strength of_him, 10 whenever he_may_come to_be_glorified_in in the holy ones of_him, and to_be_Rx-marvelled-Rx among all the ones having_believed, because was_believed the testimony of_us to you_all in day that.
   11 For which also we_are_praying always for you_all, in_order_that you_all may_consider_worthy of_the calling the god of_us, and may_fulfill every good_pleasure of_goodness, and work of_faith with power, 12 so_that may_be_glorified_in the name of_the master of_us, G‹H‹Yēsous, in you_all, and you_all in him, according_to the grace of_the god of_us and of_the_master G‹H‹Yēsous chosen_one.
  2  And we_are_asking you_all, brothers, concerning the coming of_the master of_us, G‹H‹Yēsous chosen_one, and of_us gathering_together to him, 2 in_order that not quickly to_be_shaken you_all in mind, nor you_all_are_being_alarmed, neither by spirit, nor by word, nor by letter, as_though by us, as that has_presented the day of_the master.
   3 Not someone you_all may_deceive in not_one manner, because if not may_come the apostasy first, and may_be_revealed the man of_lawlessness, the son of_destruction, 4 the one opposing and exalting himself over every being_called a_god or object_of_worship, so_as him in the temple of_god to_sit_down, demonstrating himself that he_is god.
   5 Not you_all_are_remembering that still being with you_all, these things I_was_saying to_you_all.
   6 And now the thing restraining you_all_have_known, in_order that to_be_revealed him in the of_him time.
   7 For the mystery already is_working of_lawlessness, only the one restraining it now there_is, until out_of the_midst it_may_become.
   8 And then will_be_being_revealed the lawless one, whom the master G‹H‹Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a)›H›G will_be_killing with_the spirit of_the mouth of_him, and will_be_nullifying by_the appearing of_the coming of_him, 9 of_whom is coming, according_to the_working of_G‹H‹Satan/(Sāţān)›H›G in every power, and signs, and wonders of_falsehood, 10 and in every seduction of_unrighteousness, in_the ones perishing, for which the love of_the truth not they_received, in_order that to_be_saved them.
   11 And because_of this, is_sending to_them god a_working of_deception, in_order that to_believe them in_the falsehood, 12 in_order_that may_be_judged all the ones not having_believed in_the truth, but having_taken_pleasure in_unrighteousness.
   13 But we ought to_be_giving_thanks to_god always concerning you_all, brothers having_been_loved by the the_master, that chose you_all god, from the_beginning to salvation in the_holiness of_the_spirit and faith of_the_truth, 14 to which he_called us, through the gospel of_us, to the_procuring of_the_glory of_the master of_us, G‹H‹Yēsous chosen_one/messiah.
   15 Therefore consequently, brothers, be_standing_firm and be_taking_hold_of the traditions that you_all_were_taught, whether by word, or by letter from_us.
   16 And himself the master of_us, chosen_one, G‹H‹Yēsous, and god the father of_us, the one having_loved us and having_given comfort eternal, and hope good by grace, 17 might_comfort the hearts, of_you_all and might_strengthen you_all in every work and word good.
  3  For_the rest, be_praying, brothers, for us, in_order_that the word of_the master may_be_spreading and may_be_being_glorified, as also with you_all, 2 and in_order_that we_may_be_rescued from perverse and evil people, for not is of_all are the faith.
   3 But faithful is the master, who will_be_strengthening you_all, and will_be_keeping you_all from the evil one.
   4 And we_have_persuaded in the_master to you_all that the things we_are_commanding, you_all_are_doing and will_be_doing.
   5 And the master might_direct of_you_all the hearts into the love of_god, and into the endurance of_the chosen_one.
   6 And we_are_commanding to_you_all, brothers, in the_name of_the master of_us, G‹H‹Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a)›H›G chosen_one, to_be_avoiding you_all from every brother lazily walking, and not according_to the tradition which they_received from us.
   7 For yourselves you_all_have_known how it_is_fitting to_be_imitating us, because not we_loafed among you_all, 8 nor undeservedly bread we_ate from anyone, but in labor and hardship, night and day working, in_order that not to_burden anyone of_you_all.
   9 Not that not we_are_having the_right, but in_order_that ourselves an_example we_may_give to_you_all, in_order that to_be_imitating us.
   10 For even when we_were with you_all, this we_were_commanding to_you_all, that if anyone not is_willing to_be_working, not him _let_be_eating.
   11 For we_are_hearing some are walking among you_all lazily, nothing working, but meddling.
   12 And to_such we_are_commanding and are_exhorting by the_master G‹H‹Yēsous chosen_one, that with quietness working of_themselves bread they_may_be_eating.
   13 But you_all, brothers, not may_lose_heart doing_good.
   14 And if anyone not is_submitting to_the word of_us through letter, this man be_taking_note, not to_be_associating_with with_him, in_order_that he_may_be_swayed.
   15 And_yet not as an_enemy be_considering him, but be_admonishing him as a_brother.
   16 And himself the master of_peace, might_give to_you_all peace through all time in every manner.
   The master be with all of_you_all.
   17 The greeting in_my own hand, of_G‹Paulos›G, which is my_sign in every letter, thus I_am_writing.
   18 The grace of_the master of_us, G‹H‹Yēsous chosen_one/messiah, with all of_you_all.
