Galatikoss

1  Paulos, an_ambassador (not from humans, nor through human_origin, but through Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one/messiah, and god the_father having_raised him from the_dead), 2 and the ones with me all the_brothers, to_the assemblies of_Galatia: 3 grace to_you_all and peace from god the_father and the_master of_us Yēsous chosen_one, 4 the one having_given himself concerning the sins of_us, so_that he_may_rescue us out_of the age having_presented evil, according_to the will of_the god and father of_us, 5 to_whom is the glory to the ages of_the ages.
Truly.
6 I_am_marvelling that thus quickly, you_all_are_replacing from the one having_called you_all in the_grace of_chosen_one, to a_another gospel, 7 which not is another one, except some are who disturbing you_all, and wanting to_alter the gospel of_the chosen_one.
8 But even if we or an_angel out_of heaven you_all may_be_gospel_preaching contrary_to what we_gospel_preached to_you_all, a_curse him_let_be.
9 As we_have_previously_said, even now again I_am_saying, if anyone to_you_all is_gospel_preaching contrary_to what you_all_received, a_curse him_let_be.
10 For now people I_am_persuading or god.
Or I_am_seeking to_people to_be_bringing_pleasure.
If still to_people I_was_bringing_pleasure, of_chosen_one a_slave not I_was.
11 For I_am_making_known to_you_all, brothers, the gospel having_been_gospel_preached by me, that not it_is according_to human_origin.
12 For neither I from human_origin received it, nor I_was_taught it, but by a_revelation of_Yēsous chosen_one.
13 For you_all_heard my conduct once in Judaism, that as excess I_was_persecuting the assembly of_god, and was_persecuting it.
14 And I_was_progressing in Judaism beyond many contemporaries in the descent of_me, more_abundantly zealous being of_the paternal of_me traditions.
15 But when took_pleasure the one, having_set_apart me from the_womb of_the_mother of_me, and having_called me by the grace of_him, 16 to_reveal the son of_him in me, in_order_that I_may_be_gospel_preaching him among the pagans, immediately not I_consulted with_flesh and blood, 17 nor I_went_away to Hierousalēm/(Yərūshālam) to the before me ambassadors, but I_went_away into Arabia, and again I_returned to Damaskos/(Dammeseq).
18 Then after three years, I_went_up to Hierousalēm to_become_acquainted with_Kēfas, and I_remained_on with him fifteen days.
19 But other of_the ambassadors not I_saw, except Yakōbos/(Ya'aqov), the brother of_the master.
20 And what I_am_writing to_you_all, see, before god, that not I_am_lying.
21 Then I_came into the regions of_Suria/('Arām) and Kilikia.
22 And I_was being_not_known by_face to_the assemblies of_Youdaia, in chosen_one/messiah, 23 but only hearing they_were, The one persecuting us once, now is_gospel_preaching the faith, which once he_was_persecuting.
24 And they_were_glorifying in me god.
2  Then through fourteen years, again I_went_up to Hierousalēm with Barnabas, having_taken_along_with also Titos, 2 and I_went_up according_to a_revelation, and I_placed_before before_them the gospel that I_am_proclaiming among the pagans, by but myself to_the ones supposing, lest somehow in vain I_may_be_running or I_ran.
3 But not_even Titos who was with me, a_Hellēn being, was_compelled to_be_circumcised.
4 But because_of the sneaked_in false_brothers, who slipped_in to_spy_out the freedom of_us, which we_are_having in chosen_one Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a), in_order_that us they_will_be_enslaving, 5 to_whom not_even for an_hour we_yielded in_submission, in_order_that the truth of_the gospel may_continue with you_all.
6 But of the ones supposing to_be something (what_kind once they_were, nothing to_me is_carrying_value, the_appearance god of_a_person not is_receiving), for to_me the ones supposing nothing they_contributed.
7 But instead having_seen that I_have_been_entrusted the gospel of_the uncircumcision, as Petros of_the circumcision 8 (for the one having_worked in_Petros for commission of_the circumcision, worked in_also_me toward the pagans), 9 and having_known the grace having_been_given to_me, Yakōbos and Kēfas, and Yōannēs, the ones supposing pillars to_be, the_right hands they_gave to_me and Barnabas of_fellowship, in_order_that we may_go to the pagans, and they to the circumcision, 10 only about_the poor that we_may_be_remembering, that also I_was_earnest same thing this to_do.
11 But when came Kēfas to Antioⱪeia, to his_face against_him I_resisted, because having_been_condemned he_was.
12 Before for the time to_come some from Yakōbos, with the pagans he_was_eating_with, but when he_came, he_was_shrinking_back and was_setting_apart himself, fearing the ones of the_circumcision.
13 And were_acting_hypocritically with_him the rest of_the_Youdaiōns, so_that even Barnabas was_carried_away of_them by_the hypocrisy.
14 But when I_saw that not they_are_walking_straight with the truth of_the gospel, I_said to_Kēfas before all: If you a_Youdaios, being paganly and not Youdaiōns are_living, how the pagans you_are_compelling to_be_Judaizing.
15 We are by_nature Youdaiōns and not of the_pagans sinners, 16 but having_known that not is_being_justified a_person by works of_law, if not through faith in_Yēsous chosen_one/messiah, even we in Yēsous chosen_one believed, in_order_that we_may_be_justified by faith in_chosen_one, and not by works of_law, because by the_works of_law, not will_be_being_justified any flesh.
17 But if seeking to_be_justified in chosen_one, we_were_found also ourselves sinners, consequently is chosen_one of_sin a_servant.
Never it_might_become.
18 For if that I_torn_down, these things again I_am_building, a_transgressor myself I_am_demonstrating.
19 For I through the_law to_the_law died_off, in_order_that to_god I_may_live.
˱With˲_chosen_one I_have_been_impaled_on_a_stake_with, 20 and I_am_living, no_longer it_is I, but is_living in me chosen_one, and what now I_am_living in the_flesh, in faith I_am_living in_the of_god, and chosen_one, the one having_loved me, and having_given_over himself for me.
21 Not I_am_rejecting the grace of_god, for if through the_law righteousness is, consequently chosen_one undeservedly died_off.
3  Oh foolish Galatikoss.
Who you_all bewitched, to_whom by your_eyes Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one was_publicly_portrayed having_been_impaled_on_a_stake.
2 This only I_am_wanting to_learn from you_all: by works of_law the spirit you_all_received, or by hearing with_faith.
3 Thus foolish you_all_are.
Having_begun in_the_spirit, now in_the_flesh you_all_are_accomplishing.
4 So_many things you_all_suffered vainly, if surely also vainly.
5 Therefore the one supplying to_you_all the spirit and working miracles among you_all, by the_works of_law or by hearing with_faith is_it.
6 As Abra'am/('Avərāhām) believed in_god and it_was_reckoned to_him for righteousness.
7 Be_knowing consequently that the ones of faith, these the_sons are of_Abra'am.
8 And having_foreseen the scripture that by faith is_justifying the pagans god, he_foretold_the_gospel to_Abra'am, will_be_being_blessed in you all the nations.
9 So_then the ones of faith are_being_blessed with the believing Abra'am.
10 For as_many_as of the_works of_law are under a_curse are, for it_has_been_written, cursed is everyone who not is_remaining_in in_all the things having_been_written in the scroll of_the law, to_do them.
11 And that by the_law, no_one is_being_justified before god evident it_is, because: the righteous by faith will_be_living.
12 And the law not is of faith, but: the one having_done them, will_be_living by them.
13 chosen_one/messiah us redeemed from the curse of_the law, having_become for us a_curse, because it_has_been_written: cursed is everyone having_hanged on a_tree, 14 in_order_that to the pagans the blessing of_Abra'am may_become in chosen_one of_Yēsous, in_order_that the promise of_the spirit we_may_receive through faith.
15 Brothers, according_to human_origin I_am_speaking.
Likewise of_a_human having_been_confirmed a_covenant, no_one is_rejecting or is_adding_conditions.
16 And to_Abra'am were_spoken the promises and to_the seed of_him.
Not he_is_saying: And to_the seeds, as concerning many, but as concerning one: and to_the seed of_you, which is chosen_one.
17 And this I_am_saying: the_covenant having_been_previously_ratified by god, the after four_hundred and thirty years having_become law not is_annulling, as_in_order that to_nullify the promise.
18 For if by the_law the inheritance is, no_longer it_is by a_promise, but to_Abra'am through a_promise has_granted it god.
19 Therefore why the law.
The transgressions for_reason_of it_was_added, until of_which may_come the seed to_whom he_has_been_promised, having_been_directed through angels in the_hand of_a_mediator.
20 And the mediator for_one party not is, but god one is.
21 Therefore the law is against the promises of_god.
Never it_might_become.
For if was_given a_law being_able to_give_life, really by the_law would was righteousness.
22 But imprisoned the scripture the things all under sin, in_order_that the promise by faith in_Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) chosen_one may_be_given to_the ones believing.
23 Before but the time to_come faith, under the_law we_were_being_guarded, being_imprisoned to the going faith to_be_revealed.
24 So_that the law, tutor of_us became to chosen_one, in_order_that by faith we_may_be_justified.
25 But having_come faith, no_longer under a_tutor we_are.
26 For all sons of_god you_all_are through faith in chosen_one Yēsous.
27 For as_many_as to chosen_one were_immersed, chosen_one you_all_dressed_in.
28 Neither there_is Youdaios nor Hellēn, neither there_is slave nor free, neither there_is male and female, for all you_all are in of_chosen_one/messiah Yēsous.
29 And if you_all are of_chosen_one, consequently of_Abra'am/('Avərāhām) the_seed you_all_are, according_to the_promise heirs.
4  And I_am_saying, for as_long_as time as_the heir an_infant is, nothing he_is_carrying_value from_a_slave, master of_all things being, 2 but under guardians he_is and managers, until the appointed_time by_his father.
3 Thus also we, when we_were infants, under the principles of_the world we_were having_been_enslaved.
4 But when came the fullness of_the time, sent_away god the son of_him, having_become of woman, having_become under law, 5 in_order_that the ones under the_law he_may_redeem, in_order_that the adoption_as_sons we_may_take_back.
6 And because you_all_are sons, sent_away god the spirit of_the son of_him into the hearts of_us crying_out: father, father.
7 So_as no_longer you_are a_slave, but a_son, and if a_son, also an_heir through god.
8 But then on_one_hand not having_known god, you_all_served for_the ones by_nature not being gods, 9 on_the_other_hand now having_known god, and rather having_been_known by god, how you_all_are_turning_back again to the weak and poor principles, for_which again again to_be_serving you_all_are_wanting.
10 Days you_all_are_observing, and months, and seasons, and years.
11 I_am_fearing for_you_all, lest somehow vainly I_have_labored for you_all.
12 Be_becoming as I am, because also_I am as you_all are, brothers, I_am_beseeching of_you_all.
Nothing me you_all_did_wrong, 13 but you_all_have_known that because_of weakness of_the flesh, I_gospel_preached to_you_all earlier.
14 And the trial of_you_all in the flesh of_me, not you_all_scorned nor you_all_disdained, but as a_messenger of_god you_all_received me, as chosen_one Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a).
15 Therefore is where the blessing of_you_all.
For I_am_testifying to_you_all that if possible the eyes of_you_all having_dug_out, you_all_gave them to_me.
16 So_as enemy of_you_all I_have_become, being_truthful to_you_all.
17 They_are_being_zealous for_you_all not rightly, but to_exclude you_all they_are_willing, in_order_that for_them you_all_may_be_being_zealous.
18 But good it_is you_all_are_being_zealous in a_good thing always, and not only in the time to_be_being_present me with you_all.
19 Children of_me, whom again I_am_laboring_in_birth, until of_which may_be_formed chosen_one/messiah in you_all, 20 but I_was_wanting to_be_being_present with you_all now, and to_change the voice of_me, because I_am_perplexing in you_all.
21 Be_telling to_me, you_all under the_law wanting to_be, the law not you_all_are_hearing.
22 For it_has_been_written that Two Abra'am/('Avərāhām) sons had, one of the servant_girl, and one of the free woman.
23 But the one of the servant_girl, according_to flesh has_been_born, on_the_other_hand the one of the free woman, through a_promise.
24 Which things is being_allegorized, for these are two covenants: one on_one_hand from Mount Sina/(Şīnay) to slavery bearing, which is Hagar/(Hāgār).
25 and Sina Mount is in Arabia, and is_corresponding to_the present Hierousalēm/(Yərūshālam), she_is_serving for with the children of_her.
26 On_the_other_hand above Hierousalēm free is, who is the_mother of_us.
27 For it_has_been_written: be_gladdened, barren woman, you not bearing, break_forth and shout, you not laboring_in_birth, because many are the children of_the desolate more, than of_the woman having the husband.
28 And you_all brothers, as Isa'ak/(Yiʦəḩāq), of_promise children are.
29 But as then the one according_to flesh having_been_born, was_persecuting the one according_to spirit, thus also now it_is.
30 But what is_saying the scripture.
Cast_out the servant_girl and the son of_her, for no not will_be_inheriting the son of_the servant_girl, with the son of_the free woman.
31 Therefore brothers, not we_are of_the_servant_girl children, but of_the free woman.
5  in_freedom, us chosen_one freed, therefore be_standing_firm, and not again in_a_yoke of_slavery be_being_entangled.
2 See I Paulos am_saying to_you_all that if you_all _may_be_being_circumcised, chosen_one you_all nothing will_be_benefiting.
3 And I_am_testifying again to_every man being_circumcised that a_debtor he_is, all the law to_perform.
4 You_all_were_released from chosen_one, whoever in the_law are_being_justified, from_the grace you_all_fell_from.
5 For we by_the_spirit by faith the_hope of_righteousness are_eagerly_waiting.
6 For in chosen_one Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a), neither circumcision anything is_prevailing, nor uncircumcision, but faith through love working.
7 You_all_were_running well, who you_all hindered, by_the_truth not to_be_being_persuaded.
8 The persuasion is not of the one calling you_all.
9 A_little leaven whole the lump is_leavening.
10 I have_trusted in you_all in the_master that not_one other thing you_all_will_be_thinking.
But the one disturbing you_all, will_be_bearing the judgment, whoever he_may_be.
11 But I, brothers, if circumcision still I_am_proclaiming, why still I_am_being_persecuted.
Consequently has_been_nullified the offense of_the cross.
12 I_wish also will_be_cutting_off themselves the ones upsetting you_all.
13 For you_all for freedom were_called, brothers, only not use the freedom for an_opportunity to_the flesh, but through love be_serving for_one_another.
14 For the whole one law in word has_been_fulfilled, in that: you_will_be_loving the neighbor of_you as yourself.
15 But if one_another you_all_are_biting and you_all_are_devouring, be_watching_out, lest by one_another you_all_may_be_consumed_up.
16 But I_am_saying, by_the_spirit be_walking, and the_desire of_the_flesh by_no_means not may_accomplish.
17 For the flesh is_desiring against the spirit, and the the_spirit against the flesh, these for to_one_another is_opposing, in_order_that not whatever things you_all_may_be_wanting, these things you_all_may_be_doing.
18 But if by_the_spirit you_all_are_being_led, not you_all_are under the_law.
19 And manifest is the works of_the flesh, which is sexual_immorality, impurity, wantonness, 20 idolatry, sorcery, enmities, strife, jealousies, rages, selfish_ambitions, dissensions, sects, 21 envies, drunkennesses, carousings, and similar things to_these, which I_am_previously_saying to_you_all, as I_previously_said, that the ones such things doing, the_kingdom of_god not will_be_inheriting.
22 But the fruit of_the spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faith, 23 gentleness, self-control, against such things, no there_is law.
24 And the ones of_chosen_one/messiah Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a), the flesh impaled_on_a_stake with its passions and desires.
25 If we_are_living by_the_spirit, by_the_spirit also we_may_be_aligning.
26 Not we_may_be_becoming conceited, one_another provoking, one_another envying.
6  Brothers, if even may_be_previously_caught a_person in a_certain transgression, you_all, the spiritual ones, be_preparing such man in a_spirit of_gentleness, looking_out yourself, lest also you may_be_tempted.
2 Of_one_another the burdens be_bearing, and thus fulfill the law of_the chosen_one.
3 For if is_supposing anyone to_be something, nothing being, he_is_deceiving himself.
4 But the work of_himself let_be_approving and then for himself only the boast will_be_having, and not for another.
5 For each his own burden will_be_bearing.
6 And let_be_sharing the one being_instructed in_the word, with_the one instructing him, in all good things.
7 Not be_being_misled, god not is_being_snubbed.
For whatever thing may_be_sowing a_person, this also he_will_be_reaping.
8 Because the one sowing to the flesh of_himself, from the flesh will_be_reaping corruption, but the one sowing to the spirit, from the spirit will_be_reaping life eternal.
9 And good doing, not we_may_be_losing_heart, for in_time our_own, we_will_be_reaping not being_exhausted.
10 Therefore consequently, as time we_are_having, we_may_be_working good toward all, and most_of_all toward the household of_the of_faith.
11 See in_how_much to_you_all letters I_wrote with_my own hand.
12 As_many_as are_wanting to_appear_good in the_flesh, these are_compelling you_all to_be_being_circumcised, only in_order_that for_the cross of_the chosen_one Yēsous not they_are_persecuting.
13 For and_not the ones having_been_circumcised they the_law are_keeping, but they_are_willing you_all to_be_being_circumcised, in_order_that in the your flesh they_may_boast.
14 But for_me, never it_might_become to_be_boasting, except in the cross of_the master of_us, Yēsous chosen_one, through whom to_me the_world has_been_impaled_on_a_stake, and_I to_the_world.
15 For neither circumcision anything is, nor uncircumcision, but new creation.
16 And as_many_as by_principle this will_be_aligning, peace be on them, and mercy, and on the Israēl/(Yisərā'ēl) of_god.
17 Of_the rest, troubles to_me no_one let_be_bringing_about, for I the marks of_Yēsous/(Yəhōshū'a) on the body of_me am_bearing.
18 The grace of_the master of_us, Yēsous chosen_one/messiah, with the spirit of_you_all be, brothers.
Truly.
