Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text in advance before using in public. Some things (like capitalisation of “him” referring to Jesus or “father” referring to God) in the RV haven’t been decided yet so we’re still testing both ways.

Note: The Readers’ Version on the left is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 16th century English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version on the right which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to fully understand the Literal Version.) By comparing the left and right columns, you should be able to easily get the message of the text, while at the same time keeping an eye on what it was actually translated from.

It is our prayer that the Open English Translation of the Bible will give you clear understanding of the accounts and messages written by the God-inspired Biblical writers.

Introduction

The baseen of Hub, sabeka this story concerning the person matareng impasalerep te mangune-kune karereetan—migpatey ka langun anak din, naawe ka langun karatuan din, and miggingumaan pad iya again sikandin te mangune-kune dalu. Ne due pitu baad te story te this baseen. Ne pinaahi te sika me story, impanengneng te talagsulat ke nekey ka inggeram of Hub and te me alukuy rin concerning the sika me karereetan. Ne ka impurtanti baad te story, iyan sika se paahi te God te peg-uyan te keet-etawan din. Ne nataman, migpapitew mismu ka God ki Hub.

Te marani pad te first te this baseen, ingkahiyan of Meibulan ka God te kene meupiya ka peg-uyan te keet-etawan din egdasahan din ka me matareng. Purisu diye egpangkus te iyan naan de egpuunan eggimu te matareng ka people tenged te dasag. Ne kenad e egpuun te pusung dan ka peggimu ran te matareng. Ne this iya ka ingkahi of Meibulan concerning ki Hub. Purisu intuhutan te God si Meibulan eg-el-elehan ka pegkamatinumanen of Hub ke egpabulus-bulus naan pad sikandin matareng ke eg-antus sikandin.

Ne pinaahi te karaan pegpalintutuu, impasabut te me alukuy of Hub mig-antus sikandin tenged su nakasale sikandin. Piru for ki Hub, kene perem eleg eg-antus sikandin te sika legpad su matareng ma sikandin person. Ware din nasabut ke mania te intuhutan te God eggingumaan sikandin te makaliyas-liyas dalu te matareng ma sikandin person. Purisu inhanggat din ka God eggendiye sikandan te hukumanan. Ne minsan pad due, ware naawei ka pegpalintutuu of Hub, piru igkeupii rin eg-ileen sikandin matareng te God, and egkaangken din ded ka kabantug isip matareng person.

Ware tabaka te God ka me inse of Hub. Piru inggehet ded te God ka pegpalintutuu of Hub, pinaahi te geem and katuenan din. Ne in-ila of Hub labew matagseb ka God, duma te pegpariralem din, and migbuyu sikandin te pasaylu te nenallalag din puun te langet din.

Ne ka katamanan te this baseen, migpanengneng impalibed ded ki Hub ka meupiya keugpe din, and migsubla pad sikandin matubung du te dengan. Ne impangehetan te God ka me alukuy of Hub, su ware ma sikandan nakanengneng ke mania te mig-antus si Hub. Si Hub de ka nakasabut madmaresen pad ka God du te karaan pegpalintutuu.
