Introduction

Si Danyil ka migsulat te seini baseen. Sabeka sikandin matig-Huda indakep peendiye te Babilunya te lukes-lukes pad sikandin. Insulat din seini te timpu te mig-antus amana ka me Hudiyu te pegbayad-bayad and pegdeeg-deeg te Hari migsimba te false gods. Nasulat kayi ka concerning the pegkeyimu of Danyil labew te me upisyal diye te Babilunya and ka pegkamatinumanen din te pegpamakey te God te timpu te pegmandu enni Hari Nibukadnisar, Bilsasar, Daryu, and Sirus. Ne nasulat degma kayi ka concerning the tatelu me alukuy rin ensi Sadrak, Misak, and Abidnigu.

Nasulat degma kayi ka immakita te God ki Danyil migtagne concerning the pegkamaresen and pegkahuhus te duma nations bunsud te Babilunya. Intagne degma kayi ke nekey ka egkeyitabu te mewuri me timpu.

Nasulat pad man‑e te seini baseen iyan egbuut ka God te langun egkeyitabu kayi te ampew te tane. Sikandin ka eggimu te nations and sikandin ded degma ka egdereet kayi. Sikandin degma ka egtanggu te keet-etawan din and egpamatarengen din ka world te mewuri me timpu.
