Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text in advance before using in public. Some things (like capitalisation of “him” referring to Jesus or “father” referring to God) in the RV haven’t been decided yet so we’re still testing both ways.

Note: The Readers’ Version on the left is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 16th century English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version on the right which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to fully understand the Literal Version.) By comparing the left and right columns, you should be able to easily get the message of the text, while at the same time keeping an eye on what it was actually translated from.

Titus

It is our prayer that the Open English Translation of the Bible will give you clear understanding of the accounts and messages written by the God-inspired Biblical writers.

Introduction

Si Titu, kene sikandin Hudiyu and kene egpalintutuu dengan te God. Piru hengkayi te mewuri e timpu na-Kristuwanen e sikandin. Ne puun dutu, neyimu sikandin talagbulig of Paul te me himu rin. This sulat, impeuyan of Paul diye te kandin te inggaat din diye te Krita eyew eg-uyan te punduk te believers. (The Krita, pulu te dahat sakup te Grisya.)

Ne due tatelu impanengneng te this sulat: The first, impasuman-suman si Titu ke nekey ka eleg egbatasanen te me pangulu te believers. Keilangan egpakataha sikandin here tenged su masulug ka mareet batasan te matig-Krita and due masulug talagpanulu kene malehet egpasuwey te believers.

The igkarangeb, intambahan si Titu ke egmenuwen din te teaching ka nalein-lein people diye te punduk te believers, lagkes seeye se me lukes-lukes and me uripen, and seeye se bayew e me buyag me lukes and women. (Ne iyan egpanulu te me mengebay ka buyag e women.)

Te katammanan, migtambahan of Paul si Titu concerning the Kristuwanen peg-ugpe, and pegpanalliya te peg-ehet te duma, pegpaapuley, and pegbaad-baad diye te punduk te believers. Impasuman-suman again of Paul si Titu egpakatuman ki te seeye se insuhu kanta tenged te kaluwasan imbehey kanta of Hisu Kristu.

Egkeupian si Paul egkanengnengan here te this sulat amana impurtanti diye te believers ka peggimu te meupiya (1:6; 2:7,14; 3:1,8-9,14) piru kene sika egpakaluwas (3:5).
