Note: The Readers’ Version on the left is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 16th century English. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to fully understand the Literal Version.) By comparing the left and right columns, you should be able to easily get the message of the text, while at the same time keeping an eye on what it was actually translated from.

Introduction

Author

The migsulat te this baseen, iyan si Matiyu egngaranan ded ki Libi. Sabeka sikandin te sapulu and daruwa (12) follower of Jesus, and egngaranan ki Alpiyu ka amey rin. Matig-Galilee sikandin piru diye migtrabahu te Kapirnaum isip talagsukut te buhis te ware pad sikandin neyimu follower of Jesus. Ne kema ke si Jesus buwad e ka migbalew-balew te ngaran din Libi te neyimu e sikandin follower, and migngaranan e sikandin ki Matiyu iyan igpasabut te Hebrews linalahan ‘ka dasag te God.’

This account

The story of Matiyu, diye started te itulan te kaap-apuan of Jesus, te birth rin, pegbewutismu and te pegtintal kandin, te pegwali and te teaching din, and te healing din te egmanderalu diye te Galilee. Pegkapenga here, nahuhud e again of Matiyu ka concerning the going of Jesus te Jerusalem, and ka nangimu rin te diyad sikandin. Nahuhud din again ka neyitabu te katamanan simana of Jesus diye te Jerusalem, ka peglansang kandin te krus, and ka pegkeuyag din.

Nahuhud te this baseen si Jesus, iyan ka Kristu promised te God save te Jews, and te langun non-Jews egpalintutuu and egsalig kandin. Ne pinaahi kandin, natuman te God ka promised din diye te keet-etawan din sumale te Tapey Kasabutan.

This se Meupiya Panugtulen, migpeila si Jesus ka mabantug Talagpanulu, due katenged din te pegpasabut te Balaud te God. Kayi te this baseen, due lalimma malayat teaching of Jesus insulat of Matiyu, and naan pa iya ka teaching of Jesus concerning the Peghari te God: (1) The pegwali diye te bubungan concerning the pegbebatasan te people, ka me katungdanan and ka egkengarawat dan, and ka egtamanan te people nasakup te Migharian te God (Kapitulu 5–7); (2) ka pegpaney-paney te sapulu and daruwa (12) apustulis concerning the me himu ran (Kapitulu 10); (3) ka me allegory concerning the Peghari te God (Kapitulu 13); (4) ka teaching concerning the kaluwasan te egkeyimu follower (Kapitulu 18); and (5) ka teaching concerning the katammanan te this timpu and ka concerning the egginguma Peghari te God. (Kapitulu 24–25).
