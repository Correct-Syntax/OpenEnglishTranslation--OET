It is our prayer that the Open English Translation of the Bible will give you clear understanding of the accounts and messages written by the God-inspired Biblical writers.

Introduction

Nahuhud te this baseen ka concerning the keet-etawan te nasud Israel te timpu si Samwil pad ka pangulu ran until nakasubal e si Saul migpangulu kandan. Ne si Saul ka first Hari. Nahuhud also te this baseen ke nekey ka egpuunan naawaan si Saul te katungdanan din isip Hari, and impasubalan e sikandin te God ki Dabid.

Te ware pad Hari dengan te Israel, iyan pad in-isip te people Hari dan ka God. Piru mighangyu sikandan te God egbehayan te Hari egpangulu kandan. Ne igkeupii te God egparuma-ruma ka Hari and ka people te pegmandu din and te kandin peggukum. Su ka langun katenged te person, meyaman ma and se egkaayu-ayu, keilangan egpekeuyun te Balaud te God.

The uluwan te this baseen sika se pegkamatinumanen te God diye egpangkus te kapanaluwan, and ka pegsupak te igkeupii te God diye egpangkus te karereetan. Naay-ayaran sika nalalag te God diye te kapitulu 2:30 miggenendue, “The migtahud keddi, egtahuren ku also and ka miglemet keddi, eglemetan ku also.”
