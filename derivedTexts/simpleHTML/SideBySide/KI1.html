Introduction

The first baseen concerning the me Hari sumpul te story concerning the migharian te me Israilita started te baseen of Samwil. Nabaad this te tatelu: 1.) The pegsubal of Solomon isip Hari te nasud te Israel, and ka pegpatey te amey rin si Dabid. 2.) The peghari of Solomon and ka me kalampusan din, labi en iya ka peggimu te temple te Jerusalem. 3.) The pegkabaad te migharian te Israel te daruwa: ka migharian dapit te igkahibang balabahan, and ka migharian dapit te igkakawanan balabahan, and ka me story concerning the me Hari migpangulu kandan until siyam gatus and lalimma pulu (950) leg‑un te ware pad neetew si Jesus.

Kayi te daruwa Baseen concerning the me Hari, inggukuman ka tagse pangulu sumale te pegkamatinumanen dan te God, and nanengnengan ka kalampusan te intiru nasud egpakaparuma-ruma te pegkamatinumanen dan. The pegsimba te me false gods and ka pegkamasinupaken, egpakabehey te karereetan. Ne ware miglampus te this peg-eleg-eleg ka langun Hari te migharian dapit te igkahibang balabahan. Piru due mule miglampus me Hari te migharian dapit te igkakawanan balabahan, and due also ware miglampus.

Ne ka amana neneila people te An-anayan baseen concerning the me Hari, iyan ka me prupita te Master God mabbagget me talagpangguhud te lalag te God migpaney-paney te people kene egsimba te me false gods, and kene egsupak te God. Labi en iya si Ilyas and ka panugtulen concerning the pegkuntere din te me prupita of Baal (kapitulu 18).
