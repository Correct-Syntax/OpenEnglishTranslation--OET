Some things (like capitalisation of “him” referring to Jesus or “father” referring to God) in the RV haven’t been decided yet so we’re still testing both ways.

Note: The Readers’ Version on the left is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version on the right which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to fully understand the Literal Version.) By comparing the left and right columns, you should be able to easily get the message of the text, while at the same time keeping an eye on what it was actually translated from.

Introduction

The seini baseen concerning the intagne dengan of prupita Husiyas anak of Biiri te me walu gatus (800) leg‑un te ware pad neetew si Jesus. Nekeg-iling si prupita Husiyas and si Amus migpanagne diye te people te iling ded timpu. Piru migpabulus-bulus mule migpanagne si Husiyas te nenakasinundul pad me leg‑un. Te seeye timpu, nabaad te daruwa ka inged te Israel and diye migwali si Husiyas te ampew baad senge-kakuwa egngaranan din te Israel, and senge-kakuwa egngaranan man‑e te Ipraim. Ipraim ka ngaran te amana impurtanti tribu diye te ampew baad. Ne ka Samarya ka iney siyudad. Ne ka deisek baad te inged te Israel, ingngaranan din te Huda. Migwali sikandin te ware pad dereeti ka Samarya te miglusud me kuntere dan.

Te seini baseen, impapitew of Husiyas ka pegsupak te keet-etawan te Magbebaye God and ka peg-engked dan kandin. Egpekeiling sikandan te masinupaken asawa rin si Gumir and mig-engked kandin. Ne amana nalaggew si Husiyas tenged te pegsupak te people and migsimba te me false gods, and warad e migsalig te Magbebaye God. Piru te katammanan, iyan ded iya egdapit ka geyinawa te God te keet-etawan din and egpakalibed ded man‑e sikandan diye te kandin. Dakel amana ka geyinawa rin kandan, and impanengneng din seini kayi te amana makalulunu me lalag, “Egmenuwen ku sikeykew te eg-engked, he Ipraim? Kene ku iya sikeykew egbalaharen egkatalu, he Israel. Kene ku sikeykew egdereetan iling te Adma and Sibuwim. Kene egpakatuhut ka pusung ku te iling due, su dakel ka geyinawa ku keykew.” (11:8).
