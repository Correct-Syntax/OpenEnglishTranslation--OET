Introduction

Si Isikil priest dengan there to Jerusalem and sabeka sikandin of naddakep impanguyan there to Babilunya of not yet naahew the Jerusalem, until naahew e this of leg‑un 586, of not yet born the the messiah. Purisu diyad sikandin nekeugpe of Babilunya and neyimu e sikandin prupita of igkalalimma leg‑un puun of pegkarakep din. The lalag din for of that people naddakep diyad of Babilunya and of that there to Jerusalem. The document of Isikil nabaad of hen‑em: 1.) The calling of God ki Isikil isip prupita; 2.) the warnings there to people concerning the legpad of God kandan, and of egginguma pegkahuhus of Jerusalem; 3.) the me lalag of God concerning the legpad igpasalerep din of various nasud migdeeg-deeg and migpanulu of me Israilita of pegpakasale; 4.) the me pegliwang for of Israel of naahew e of enemies the Jerusalem, and the saad concerning the egginguma good keugpe; 5.) the pegkatalu of langun enemy of Israel; 6.) the pegpahunsiling of Isikil of temple and of nasud nakalibed of keugpe dengan here.

Dakel very the faith of Isikil, and malantip the me suman-suman din. Kasuluhan of me suman-suman din, migginguma pinaahi of immakita. Ne kasuluhan of me lalag din, impapitew pinaahi of work due igpasabut, and nakita this of langun. Impapitew of Isikil the keimpurtanti of peg-new of pusung and of panisingan, and it’s necessary eg-inniyug the tagse person of sins din mismu. Migpangguhud again si Isikil concerning the pegpallateng of new keugpe of Israel. Ne isip prupita and priest, due dakel kabagget din for of temple, and of me keilanganen so that egkamatulus.
