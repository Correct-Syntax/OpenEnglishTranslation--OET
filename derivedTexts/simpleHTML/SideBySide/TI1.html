Some things (like capitalisation of “him” referring to Jesus or “father” referring to God) in the RV haven’t been decided yet so we’re still testing both ways.

Note: The Readers’ Version on the left is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 16th century English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version on the right which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to fully understand the Literal Version.) By comparing the left and right columns, you should be able to easily get the message of the text, while at the same time keeping an eye on what it was actually translated from.

1 Timothy

It is our prayer that the Open English Translation of the Bible will give you clear understanding of the accounts and messages written by the God-inspired Biblical writers.

Introduction

Si Timutiyu, sabeka Kristuwanen diye egpuun te Asya. Hudiyu ka iney rin Grigu mule ka amey rin. Tenged su nakita of Paul malehet ka pegpalintutuu of Timutiyu, inggimu rin sikandin duma rin and bate-bate din te pegwali and te pegpangguhud te Meupiya Panugtulen (Me Himu 16:1-3). Ne ka Ipisu iya ka sabeka lungsud miggendiyaan dan. Ne diye ihaat of Paul si Timutiyu eyew iyan eg-uyan te believers. Te bayew e naluhey si Timutiyu diye te Ipisu, migsulatan e sikandin of Paul. Me tatelu pulu (30) buwa ka idad of Timutiyu te seeye timpu.

Due tatelu tuyu te this sulat of Paul ki Timutiyu:

1.) The first tuyu of Paul, migpaney-paney sikandin concerning the kene malehet teaching diye te punduk te believers. This teaching, nenekegsewug e te me suman-suman te Jews and te non-Jews. Su sumale kandan mareet ka this se world mig-ugpaan ta. Ne and kun de egkaluwas ka person ke due katuenan din and kene egpangeen te duma klasi te egkeenen and kene also eg-asawa. Migpanulu si Paul kene malehet sika.

2.) Migpanulu again ka this sulat concerning the peg-uyan te punduk te believers and te pegsimba. Insulat here ka eleg egbatasanen te sabeka pangulu te believers, te talagbulig te believers and te duma believers me balu and me uripen.

3.) Te katammanan, migtambahan din si Timutiyu ke egmenuwen egkeyimu meupiya suluhuanen of Hisu Kristu, and concerning the katungdanan din diye te me Kristuwanen.

Main components of this “book”

Introduction 1:1-2

The teaching concerning the punduk te believers and te me pangulu 1:3-3:16

The teaching ki Timutiyu concerning the himu rin 4:1-6:21
