Note: The Readers’ Version on the left is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 16th century English. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to fully understand the Literal Version.) By comparing the left and right columns, you should be able to easily get the message of the text, while at the same time keeping an eye on what it was actually translated from.

Introduction

The baseen te Diyuturunumiyu, impaay-ayaran megsinsinundula me story of Muwisis diye te descendants of Israel, diye te inged te Muwab, ka inged insanggelan dan puun te naluhey peggipanew ran diye te disirtu, and te timpu mahaan e sikandan egseled and eg-ugpe diye te Kanaan.

Ne this ka duma impurtanti nahuhud te this baseen: 1.) Nahuhud here ka pegpasuman-suman of Muwisis te descendants of Israel concerning the neneyitabu te nanlihad hep‑at pulu (40) leg‑un, and ka peghangyu din te descendants of Israel te pegsuman-suman ke immenu sikandan te eg-inahak te Master diye te disirtu. Ne nahuhud again here ka peghangyu of Muwisis kandan eg-ikul and egmatinumanen te God. 2.) Nahuhud again here ka pegguhud of Muwisis te Sapulu (10) Suhu and ka pegpasabut din te An-anayan Suhu, and ka pegpanengneng din te descendants of Israel keilangan iyan dan de iya egpamakayen ka Master God. Nalagkes nahuhud here ka lein-lein balaud egpeikulan te descendants of Israel diye te Kanaan. 3.) Nahuhud again here ka pegpasuman-suman of Muwisis te descendants of Israel te igpasabut te kasabutan te God diye te kandan, and ka pegbagget din kandan te pegtuman te sika kasabutan. 4.) Nahuhud again here ka peg-alam ki Huswi isip igliwan te pangulu te keet-etawan te God and ka peg-ulahing of Muwisis te peggale-gale te pegkamatinumanen te God te me saad din, and ka pegpanalangin te descendants of Israel. Nahuhud again here ka pegpatey of Muwisis diye te Muwab, talipag te Hurdan dapit te igsile.

The mabantug uluwan te this baseen iyan ka pegluwas and pegpanalangin te God te keet-etawan in-alam din and inggeyinawaan. Purisu eleg iya egsumsumanen te descendants of Israel eggeyinawaan and egtahuren ka God eyew egpakaangken sikandan te umul, and kene egkaawaan te panalangin.

The amana impurtanti me birsikulu here iyan ka 6:4-6 and sika me birsikulu ka ingkahi of Jesus labew te langun suhu miggenendue: Geyinawei niyu ka God niyu te intiru pusung, gimukud, and keseg niyu.
