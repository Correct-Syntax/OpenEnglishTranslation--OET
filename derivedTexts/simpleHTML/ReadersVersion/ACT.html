Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

Acts

Introduction

The me Himu te me Apustulis of Hisu Kristu, sumpul ded te Meupiya Panugtulen Sumale ki Lukas. The amana impurtanti tuyu te seini baseen, ka pegpangguhud ke immenu te peg-ikul ki Jesus te people first migmalintutuu kandin. The Panisingan te Magbebaye ka migbulig kandan te pegpanugtul te Meupiya Panugtulen concerning kandin “diye te Jerusalem, diye te intiru inged te Hudiya, and diye te Samarya, and te minsan hendei inged kayi te ampew te tane” (1:8).

The seini baseen, nabaad te tatelu baad, seini egpekeiling te tatelu inged inwaliyan te Meupiya Panugtulen concerning ki Kristu: (1) Nabunsuran ka me himu te me Kristuwanen diye te Jerusalem te nabatun e si Jesus diye te langit; (2) Ne seini nekeempet man‑e diye te Hudiya; and (3) diye pad man‑e te Miditiranyu taman te Ruma.

Kayi degma nasulat ka sika se himu te Panisingan te God migginguma te me Kristuwanen diye te Jerusalem te aldew te Pintikustis. Nakabulig sika and nakabehey te kanekal te people and te me pangulu te migmalintutuu dengan ingguhud te seini baseen.

Main components of this “book”

The pegpangandam te pegpamalehet 1:1-26

a. The katammanan suhu of Jesus and ka saad din 1:1-14

b. The pegliwan ki Hudas 1:15-26

The pegpamalehet diye te Jerusalem 2:1-8:3

The pegpamalehet diye te Hudiya and diye te Samarya 8:4-12:25

The neyimu of Paul 13:1-28:31

a. The first peggipanew of Paul 13:1-14:28

b. The kalibulungan diye te Jerusalem 15:1-35

c. The igkarangeb peggipanew of Paul 15:36-18:22

d. The igkatelu peggipanew of Paul 18:23-21:16

e. Naprisu si Paul diye te Jerusalem, diye te Sisarya, and diye te Ruma 21:17-28:31

It is our prayer that this Readers’ Version of the Open English Translation of the Bible will give you clear understanding of the messages written by the inspired Biblical writers.
