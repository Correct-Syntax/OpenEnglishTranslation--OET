Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

Introduction

The baseen te Numiru migpangguhud concerning the descendants of Israel seled te hep‑at pulu (40) leg‑un puun te peg-awe dan te Bubungan Sinay, taman te migginguma sikandan diye te igsile te tane insaad te God igbehey kandan. The ngaran te seini baseen, due kalabetan te amana impurtanti baad te seini pangguhuren iyan ka pegpanlista of Muwisis te descendants of Israel diye te Bubungan Sinay te ware pad sikandan mig-awe diye. Peglihad te hep‑at pulu (40) leg‑un, inggimu e man‑e ka pegpanlista diye te Muwab, dapit te igsile te Weyig Hurdan. Te elat-elat te seini se kararuwa pegpanlista te keet-etawan, miggendiye ka descendants of Israel te Kadis-Barniya te igkakawanan balabahan eletanan te Kanaan. Piru puun te Kadis-Barniya, ware sikandan nakaseled te tane insaad te God igbehey kandan. Peglihad te masulug me leg‑un te peg-ugpe dan te seeye inged, miggendiye sikandan te tane dapit te igsile te Weyig Hurdan. The sabeka punduk te keet-etawan mig-ugpe diye, and ka duma miglapas te Weyig Hurdan peendiye te Kanaan.

The baseen te Numiru migpangguhud man‑e concerning the keet-etawan layun naawei te kabagget, nenaaldek taliware te kalised, and migsukul te God and ki Muwisis in-alam te God eyew iyan egpangulu kandan. Migpangguhud man‑e seini concerning the pegkamatinumanen te God and te pegsagman din te keet-etawan din ware egtamanan minsan pad nenaawaan sikandan te kabagget, and migmasinupaken. Migpangguhud man‑e seini concerning the kene egkataman pegpamakey of Muwisis te God and te keet-etawan din minsan pad te due me timpu naaminan sikandin te pegpariralem.
