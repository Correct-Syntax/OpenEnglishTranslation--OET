Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text in advance before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 16th century English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

Ezekiel

Introduction

Si Isikil talagpanubad dengan diye te Jerusalem and sabeka sikandin te naddakep impanguyan diye te Babilunya te ware pad naahew ka Jerusalem, until naahew e this te leg‑un 586, te ware pad neetew ka Kristu. Purisu diyad sikandin nekeugpe te Babilunya and neyimu e sikandin prupita te igkalalimma leg‑un puun te pegkarakep din. The lalag din for te seeye se people naddakep diyad te Babilunya and te seeye se diye te Jerusalem. The baseen of Isikil nabaad te hen‑em: 1.) The calling te God ki Isikil isip prupita; 2.) ka me paney-paney diye te keet-etawan concerning the legpad te God kandan, and te egginguma pegkahuhus te Jerusalem; 3.) ka me lalag te God concerning the legpad igpasalerep din te nalein-lein nasud migdeeg-deeg and migpanulu te me Israilita te pegpakasale; 4.) ka me pegliwang for te Israel te naahew e te me kuntere ka Jerusalem, and ka saad concerning the egginguma meupiya keugpe; 5.) ka pegkatalu te langun kuntere te Israel; 6.) ka pegpahunsiling of Isikil te temple and te nasud nakalibed te keugpe dengan here.

Dakel amana ka pegpalintutuu of Isikil, and malantip se me suman-suman din. Kasuluhan te me suman-suman din, migginguma pinaahi te immakita. Ne kasuluhan te me lalag din, impapitew pinaahi te himu due igpasabut, and nakita this te langun. Impapitew of Isikil ka keimpurtanti te peg-iyam te pusung and te panisingan, and keilangan eg-inniyug ka tagse person te sins din mismu. Migpangguhud again si Isikil concerning the pegpallateng te iyam keugpe te Israel. Ne isip prupita and talagpanubad, due dakel kabagget din for te temple, and te me keilanganen eyew egkamatulus.
