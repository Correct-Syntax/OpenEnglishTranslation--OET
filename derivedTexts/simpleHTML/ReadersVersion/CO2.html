Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

2 Corinthians

Introduction

The igkarangeb sulat of Paul te Matig-Kurintu, diye din sulata te Masidunya. Insulat din seini te timpu kene amana meupiya ka rilasyun dan te migmalintutuu diye te Kurintu.

Kayi te first baad te seini baseen, migpangguhud si Paul concerning the rilasyun dan te migmalintutuu diye te Kurintu. Impangguhud din ka pegkasasew rin concerning the pegpeyilew and pegsupak kandin te migmalintutuu. Ne impangguhud din man‑e ka kahale din ka seeye pegkabelu din, nakapamehas te peg-inniyug te migmalintutuu te sale dan and nekeg-uli ka meupiya geyinawa ran. Ne mighangyu sikandin te migmalintutuu egpabulus egpangangey te dasag dan igbulig te migmalintutuu egkaayu-ayu diye te Jerusalem.

Te katammanan hep‑at kapitulu, impangabangan of Paul ka pegkaapustulis din te seeye se mig-angken sikandan de ka malehet apustulis. Sikandan seeye se migbayung ki Paul kene kun malehet apustulis.

Main components of this “book”

Introduction 1:1-11

The rilasyun of Paul te migmalintutuu diye te Kurintu 1:12-7:16

The pegpangabang of Paul te kandin katenged isip apustulis 10:1-12:21

The katammanan 13:1-13
