Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

Ephesians

Introduction

The seini Sulat of Paul te Matig-Ipisu, insulat din te timpu diye sikandin te prisuwan. Ne kene ka punduk te migmalintutuu re diye te Ipisu ka egpeuyanan te seini sulat, ke kene, minsan degma ka duma me punduk te migmalintutuu.

Iyan dakel tuyu of Paul kayi ka concerning the planu te God “egsabekeen din ka langun inggimu rin diye te langit and kayi te tane and igpasakup din ki Kristu” (1:10).

Diye te first baad te sulat, nasulat e ka concerning the pegsabeka. In-ay-ayaran of Paul te pegpasabut ka concerning the pamaahi te God Amey te peg-alam te keet-etawan din, te pegpasaylu and te pegluwas kandan puun te me sale dan pinaahi ki Hisu Kristu anak din.

Diye te igkarangeb baad te sulat, mighangyu si Paul te seeye se talagbasa te seini sulat, keilangan egkakita ka pegsabeka ran diye ki Kristu pinaahi te pegsabeka ran degma diye te duma.

Masalig ka inggamit of Paul allegory concerning the pegsabeka te keet-etawan te God ki Kristu: ka migmalintutuu, iyan sika ka lawa of Kristu, sikandin ka ulu; egpekeiling te sabeka house, ka batu pabunsuran, iyan iya si Kristu; egpekeiling degma sikandan te asawa woman, si Kristu ka asawa ran.

Egkanengnengan ta degma kayi ka concerning the eleg pegbebatasan te alunggun, te me taltalaanak, and te me uripen and me ahalen. Ne diye te katamanan te seini sulat, egkanengnengan ta man‑e ka concerning the imbehey te God diye te migmalintutuu egkahamit dan eyew te pegsukul te limbung of Meibulan kandan.
