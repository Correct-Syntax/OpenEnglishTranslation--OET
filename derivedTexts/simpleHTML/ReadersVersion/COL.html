Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

Colossians

Introduction

The seini sulat of Paul igpeendiye din te migmalintutuu diye te Kulusas. The Kulusas, sabeka lungsud diye te Asya Minur diye dapit te igsile te Ipisu. Ware pad nakapanumbaley diye te kandan si Paul piru amana rin e igkasasew and impeuyanan din te me talagbulig egpuun te Ipisu.

Nakarineg si Paul due me talagpanulu kene malehet egpanulu te migmalintutuu diye te Kulusas. Migpanulu sikandan te emun ke due person egkeupian egkaluwas and egkeupian egmaralem pad ka peg-ila rin te God, kene kun eleg ka pegpalintutuu rin ki Kristu. Su kahiyen pa nikandan keilangan egtuman pad sikandin te duma ingkene eggimuwen and me tulumanen. Ne keilangan man‑e egsimba sikandin te me panalihan.

Purisu insulatan of Paul ka migmalintutuu diye te Kulusas and impanengneng din sikandan te kamalehetan concerning ki Hisu Kristu. Migkahiyan din sikandan te si Jesus iya ka miggimu te langun egkakita ta, purisu labew pad iya sikandin te me panalihan, and te duma pad kene egkakita. Egkakita degma diye te kandin ka pegka-God te Amey rin, and warad e lein egpuunan te umul te migmalintutuu gawas kandin. Sikan naa, keilangan ka migmalintutuu, kene eg-ikul te me tulumanen and pegbebatasan te people, ke kene, keilangan si Kristu re iya ka eg-ikulen.
