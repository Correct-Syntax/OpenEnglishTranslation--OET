Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text in advance before using in public.

Note: This Readers' Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn't necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 500-year old English. (It's designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what's actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you're wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that's a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.)

Note that greyed words are words that the translators consider were most probably in the mind of the writer, but as none of us can double-check with the original speakers or writers, the reader is free to disagree. They are clearly marked because we have tried to be as honest / transparent as possible.

Colossians

Introduction

This letter of Paul igpeendiye din of believers there to Kulusas. The Kulusas, sabeka lungsud there to Asya Minur there dapit of igsile of Ipisu. Ware pad nakapanumbaley there to kandan si Paul but very rin e igkasasew and impeuyanan din of me talagbulig from Ipisu.

Nakarineg si Paul due teachers not true/correct egpanulu of believers there to Kulusas. Migpanulu sikandan of emun ke due person egkeupian egkaluwas and egkeupian egmaralem pad the peg-ila rin of God, not kun correct the faith rin ki the messiah. Su kahiyen pa nikandan it’s necessary egtuman pad sikandin of duma ingkene eggimuwen and me tulumanen. Ne it’s necessary again egsimba sikandin of me panalihan.

Purisu insulatan of Paul the believers there to Kulusas and impanengneng din sikandan of kamalehetan concerning ki Jesus the messiah. Migkahiyan din sikandan of si Jesus iya the miggimu of langun egkakita ta, purisu labew pad iya sikandin of me panalihan, and of duma pad not egkakita. Egkakita also there to kandin the pegka-God of Father rin, and warad e lein reason of umul of believers gawas kandin. Sikan naa, it’s necessary the believers, not eg-ikul of me tulumanen and behaviour of people, but rather, it’s necessary the Messiah just iya the eg-ikulen.
