Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text in advance before using in public.

Note: This Readers' Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn't necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 500-year old English. (It's designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what's actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you're wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that's a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.)

Note that greyed words are words that the translators consider were most probably in the mind of the writer, but as none of us can double-check with the original speakers or writers, the reader is free to disagree. They are clearly marked because we have tried to be as honest / transparent as possible.

Job

Introduction

The document of Hub, sabeka this story concerning the person righteous/honest/sensible impasalerep of mangune-kune karereetan—migpatey the langun anak din, naawe the langun karatuan din, and miggingumaan pad iya again sikandin of mangune-kune dalu. Ne due seven part of story of this document. Ne pinaahi of sika stories, impanengneng of talagsulat ke what the inggeram of Hub and of me alukuy rin concerning the sika me karereetan. Ne the important part of story, iyan that paahi of God of carrying of people din. Ne nataman, migpapitew mismu the God ki Hub.

Te marani pad of first of this document, ingkahiyan of Satan the God of not good the carrying of people din egdasahan din the me righteous/honest/sensible. Purisu there egpangkus of iyan naan just reason eggimu of righteous/honest/sensible the people tenged of gift. Ne kenad e from pusung dan the making ran of righteous/honest/sensible. Ne this iya the said by Satan concerning ki Hub. Purisu intuhutan of God si Satan eg-el-elehan the pegkamatinumanen of Hub ke continue-bulus naan pad sikandin righteous/honest/sensible ke eg-antus sikandin.

Ne pinaahi of karaan faith, impasabut of me alukuy of Hub mig-antus sikandin tenged su nakasale sikandin. But for ki Hub, not perem correct eg-antus sikandin of sika legpad su righteous/honest/sensible ma sikandin person. Ware din nasabut ke mania of intuhutan of God eggingumaan sikandin of makaliyas-liyas dalu of righteous/honest/sensible ma sikandin person. Purisu inhanggat din the God eggendiye sikandan of hukumanan. Ne minsan pad due, ware naawei the faith of Hub, but igkeupii rin eg-ileen sikandin righteous/honest/sensible of God, and egkaangken din ded the kabantug isip righteous/honest/sensible person.

Ware tabaka of God the me inse of Hub. But inggehet ded of God the faith of Hub, pinaahi of geem and katuenan din. Ne in-ila of Hub labew matagseb the God, duma of pegpariralem din, and migbuyu sikandin of pasaylu of nenallalag din puun of langet din.

Ne the final of this document, migpanengneng impalibed ded ki Hub the good keugpe din, and migsubla pad sikandin matubung du of dengan. Ne impangehetan of God the me alukuy of Hub, su ware ma sikandan nakanengneng ke mania of mig-antus si Hub. Si Hub just the nakasabut madmaresen pad the God du of karaan faith.
