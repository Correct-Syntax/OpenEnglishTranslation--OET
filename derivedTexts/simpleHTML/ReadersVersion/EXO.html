Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

Exodus

Introduction

The lalag Iksudu puun te Grigu linalahan iyan igpasabut ka “peg-awe”. Te seini baseen, nasulat ka concerning the pegpaawe te Magbebaye te keet-etawan din neuripen diye te inged egngaranan te Egypt. Migpamalehet seini ka Magbebaye iyan iya ka malehet God and labew te langun, su ware egpakabalabag kandin te igkeupii rin eggimuwen.

Seini baseen, insulatan te story concerning the neula-ula te descendants of Israel, and nabaad seini te tatelu: 1.) ka peg-uripen kandan diye te Egypt, and ka pegpaawe te Magbebaye kandan impepanguluwan din ki Muwisis in-anak-anak te sabeka prinsisa; 2.) ka pegpangabang te Magbebaye kandan te impanlupug e sikandan te me Ihiptuhanen ka egpeendiye te Bubungan Sinay, and imbehayan pad sikandan te egkakeen and egkeinum diye te bubungan; 3.) ka pegpakita te Magbebaye ki Muwisis diye te Bubungan Sinay, and imbehayan din si Muwisis te Balaud and miggimu ran te kasabutan; 4.) ka katammanan, iyan ka peggimu te balungbalung intahuan te Kasabutan and ka impaney-paney concerning the pegsimba.

The pantek nahuhud iya te seini baseen ka concerning the neyimu te God te timpu impaawe din ka neuripen keet-etawan din and impasasindeg din sikandan sabeka nasud due egkapallateng meupiya keureme.

Si Muwisis ka amana impurtanti person kayi te story and in-alam sikandin te God iyan egpangulu te keet-etawan din puun te Egypt. The amana nataheyi te people Sapulu Balaud, nasulat iya kayi egbaseen diye egkakita te kapitulu daruwa pulu (20).

Main components of this “book”

The descendants of Israel impaawe diye te Egypt 1:1-15:21

a. The neuripen diye te Egypt 1:1-22

b. The pegkeetew and pegkankanakan of Muwisis 2:1-4:31

c. The pegpakannekal pegpakig-apul of Muwisis and of Aarun te Paraun Hari te Egypt 5:1-11:10

d. The Peglihad te Panalihan and ka peg-awe te descendants of Israel diye te Egypt 12:1-15:21

The pegligkat diye te Malalab Dahat peendiye te Bubungan Sinay 15:22-18:27

The Balaud and ka kasabutan te God 19:1-24:18

The balungbalung intahuan te Kasabutan and ka impaney-paney concerning the pegsimba 25:1-40:38
