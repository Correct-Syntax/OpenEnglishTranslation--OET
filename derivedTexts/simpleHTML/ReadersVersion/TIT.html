Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

Titus

Introduction

Si Titu, kene sikandin Hudiyu and kene egpalintutuu dengan te God. Piru hengkayi te mewuri e timpu na-Kristuwanen e sikandin. Ne puun dutu, neyimu sikandin talagbulig of Paul te me himu rin. The seini sulat, impeuyan of Paul diye te kandin te inggaat din diye te Krita eyew eg-uyan te punduk te migmalintutuu. (The Krita, pulu te dahat sakup te Grisya.)

Ne due tatelu impanengneng te seini sulat: The first, impasuman-suman si Titu ke nekey ka eleg egbatasanen te me pangulu te migmalintutuu. Keilangan egpakataha sikandin kayi tenged su masulug ka mareet batasan te matig-Krita and due masulug talagpanulu kene malehet egpasuwey te migmalintutuu.

The igkarangeb, intambahan si Titu ke egmenuwen din te pegpanulu ka nalein-lein people diye te punduk te migmalintutuu, lagkes seeye se me lukes-lukes and me uripen, and seeye se bayew e me buyag me lukes and women. (Ne iyan egpanulu te me mengebay ka buyag e women.)

Te katammanan, migtambahan of Paul si Titu concerning the Kristuwanen peg-ugpe, and pegpanalliya te peg-ehet te duma, pegpaapuley, and pegbaad-baad diye te punduk te migmalintutuu. Impasuman-suman man‑e of Paul si Titu egpakatuman ki te seeye se insuhu kanta tenged te kaluwasan imbehey kanta of Hisu Kristu.

Egkeupian si Paul egkanengnengan kayi te seini sulat amana impurtanti diye te migmalintutuu ka peggimu te meupiya (1:6; 2:7,14; 3:1,8-9,14) piru kene sika egpakaluwas (3:5).
