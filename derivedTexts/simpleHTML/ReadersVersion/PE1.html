Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

1 Peter

Introduction

The first sulat of Pidru, insulat din para te migmalintutuu nekegsuwey-suwey nangugpe diye dapit te Asya Minur. The tuyu of Pidru te migsulat kayi, ka pegbagget te migmalintutuu keilangan eglig‑en sikandan taliware te masumpit pegbayad-bayad kandan tenged te pegpalintutuu ran. Impasuman-suman sikandan of Pidru concerning the kaluwasan dan, te kamatayen of Hisu Kristu, te pegkeuyag din and te peglibed din. Su nanengnengan of Pidru emun ke iyan dan layun egsumsumanen ka egpallatengen dan diye te langit, egkaantus dan iya ka langun keyirapan kayi te ampew te tane. Impanengneng din man‑e sikandan emun ke egkeehet ka pegpalintutuu ran pinaahi te peg-antus dan te me keyirapan and me kalasayan, egpakarawat sikandan te dasag te “Timpu igpakite e si Hisu Kristu.” (1:7,13)

Migbanasalan din man‑e sikandan keilangan eg-ay-ayaran dan ka batasan dan isip me keet-etawan of Kristu, and keilangan degma egparuma-ruma sikandan te seeye se due katenged egmandu kandan.

Main components of this “book”

Introduction 1:1-2

The pegpasuman-suman concerning the kaluwasan ware katamanan 1:3-12

The pegbanasal concerning the peg-ugpe matulus 1:13-2:10

The pegparuma-ruma ta te seeye se due me katenged diye te kanta 2:11-3:7

The tambag concerning the egginguma me keyirapan 3:8-4:19

The tambag para te pangulu and te migmalintutuu 5:1-11

The me pangemusta 5:12-14

It is our prayer that this Readers’ Version of the Open English Translation of the Bible will give you clear understanding of the messages written by the inspired Biblical writers.
