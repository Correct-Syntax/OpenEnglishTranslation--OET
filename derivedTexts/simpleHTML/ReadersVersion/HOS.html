Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text in advance before using in public.

Note: This Readers' Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn't necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 500-year old English. (It's designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what's actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you're wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that's a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.)

Note that greyed words are words that the translators consider were most probably in the mind of the writer, but as none of us can double-check with the original speakers or writers, the reader is free to disagree. They are clearly marked because we have tried to be as honest / transparent as possible.

Hosea

Introduction

This document concerning the intagne dengan of prupita Husiyas anak of Biiri of me walu gatus (800) leg‑un of not yet born si Jesus. Nekeg-iling si prupita Husiyas and Amus migpanagne there to people of iling ded timpu. But migpabulus-bulus mule migpanagne si Husiyas of nenakasinundul pad years. Te that timpu, nabaad of daruwa the inged of Israel and there migwali si Husiyas of ampew part senge-kakuwa named din of Israel, and senge-kakuwa named again of Ipraim. Ipraim the ngaran of very important tribu there to ampew part. Ne the Samarya the iney siyudad. Ne the deisek part of inged of Israel, ingngaranan din of Huda. Migwali sikandin of not yet dereeti the Samarya of miglusud enemies dan.

Te this document, impapitew of Husiyas the pegsupak of people of Master God and the peg-engked dan kandin. Egpekeiling sikandan of masinupaken asawa rin si Gumir and mig-engked kandin. Ne very nalaggew si Husiyas tenged of pegsupak of people and migsimba of me false gods, and warad e migsalig of Master God. But of ending, iyan ded iya egdapit the love of God of people din and egpakalibed ded again sikandan there to kandin. Dakel very the love rin kandan, and impanengneng din this here of very makalulunu me lalag, “Egmenuwen my sikeykew of eg-engked, he Ipraim? Kene my iya sikeykew egbalaharen egkatalu, he Israel. Kene my sikeykew egdereetan iling of Adma and Sibuwim. Kene egpakatuhut the pusung my of iling due, su dakel the love my keykew.” (11:8).
