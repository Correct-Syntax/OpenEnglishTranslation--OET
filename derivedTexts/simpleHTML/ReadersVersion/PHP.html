Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

Philippians

Introduction

The seini se sulat of Paul, impeuyan din diye te migmalintutuu diye te Pilipus sakup te prubinsya te Masidunya (Me Himu 16:6-40). Sika punduk te migmalintutuu ka first impes-ek of Paul diye te nasud te Ruma sakup te Iyurupa. Insulat din seini taheed te naprisu sikandin puun te pegwali rin te Meupiya Panugtulen concerning ki Hisu Kristu.

The first tuyu of Paul te migsulat te seini baseen, su egkeupian sikandin egpasalamat te migmalintutuu diye te Pilipus tenged te dasag impeuyan dan kandin eyew te pegbulig kandin te seeye timpu. The igkarangeb tuyu din te migsulat kayi, su eyew egkabagget sikandan and egkalig‑en ka pegpalintutuu ran minsan te kalised migginguma kandan and minsan degma diye te kandin. Migtambahan sikandan of Paul keilangan egpariralem sikandan iling te pegpariralem of Jesus te kayi pad sikandin te world. Impasuman-suman din man‑e sikandan tenged de te keupiya and keyid‑u te God and te pegpalintutuu ran ki Kristu naluwas sikandan. Ne kenad keilangan egsalig pad sikandan te Balaud of Muwisis and te belief(s) te people. Migsulat man‑e sikandin concerning the kahale and keupianan igbehey te God te seeye se nasabeka diye ki Kristu.
