Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text in advance before using in public.

Note: This Readers' Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn't necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 500-year old English. (It's designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what's actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you're wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that's a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.)

Note that greyed words are words that the translators consider were most probably in the mind of the writer, but as none of us can double-check with the original speakers or writers, the reader is free to disagree. They are clearly marked because we have tried to be as honest / transparent as possible.

1 Timothy

Introduction

Si Timutiyu, sabeka Kristuwanen there from Asya. Hudiyu the iney rin Grigu mule the father rin. Tenged su nakita of Paul true/correct the faith of Timutiyu, inggimu rin sikandin duma rin and bate-bate din of preaching and of pegpangguhud of good message (Me Himu 16:1-3). Ne the Ipisu iya the sabeka lungsud miggendiyaan dan. Ne there ihaat of Paul si Timutiyu so that iyan eg-uyan of believers. Te bayew e naluhey si Timutiyu there to Ipisu, migsulatan e sikandin of Paul. Me three pulu (30) buwa the idad of Timutiyu of that timpu.

Due three tuyu of this letter of Paul ki Timutiyu:

1.) The first tuyu of Paul, migpaney-paney sikandin concerning the not true/correct teaching there to group of believers. This teaching, nenekegsewug e of me suman-suman of Jews and of non-Jews. Su sumale kandan bad the this the world mig-ugpaan ta. Ne and kun just egkaluwas the person ke due katuenan din and not egpangeen of duma klasi of egkeenen and not also eg-asawa. Migpanulu si Paul not true/correct sika.

2.) Migpanulu again the this letter concerning the carrying of group of believers and of pegsimba. Insulat here the correct egbatasanen of sabeka teacher of believers, of talagbulig of believers and of duma believers widows and me uripen.

3.) Te ending, advised din si Timutiyu ke egmenuwen egkeyimu good suluhuanen of Jesus the messiah, and concerning the katungdanan din there to Christians.

Introduction 1:1-2
