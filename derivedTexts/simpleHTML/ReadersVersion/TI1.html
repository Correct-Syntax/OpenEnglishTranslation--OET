Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

1 Timothy

Introduction

Si Timutiyu, sabeka Kristuwanen diye egpuun te Asya. Hudiyu ka iney rin Grigu mule ka amey rin. Tenged su nakita of Paul malehet ka pegpalintutuu of Timutiyu, inggimu rin sikandin duma rin and bate-bate din te pegwali and te pegpangguhud te Meupiya Panugtulen (Me Himu 16:1-3). Ne ka Ipisu iya ka sabeka lungsud miggendiyaan dan. Ne diye ihaat of Paul si Timutiyu eyew iyan eg-uyan te migmalintutuu. Te bayew e naluhey si Timutiyu diye te Ipisu, migsulatan e sikandin of Paul. Me tatelu pulu (30) buwa ka idad of Timutiyu te seeye timpu.

Due tatelu tuyu te seini sulat of Paul ki Timutiyu:

1.) The first tuyu of Paul, migpaney-paney sikandin concerning the kene malehet pegpanulu diye te punduk te migmalintutuu. The seini pegpanulu, nenekegsewug e te me suman-suman te me Hudiyu and te kene me Hudiyu. Su sumale kandan mareet ka seini se world mig-ugpaan ta. Ne and kun de egkaluwas ka person ke due katuenan din and kene egpangeen te duma klasi te egkeenen and kene degma eg-asawa. Migpanulu si Paul kene malehet sika.

2.) Migpanulu man‑e ka seini sulat concerning the peg-uyan te punduk te migmalintutuu and te pegsimba. Insulat kayi ka eleg egbatasanen te sabeka pangulu te migmalintutuu, te talagbulig te migmalintutuu and te duma migmalintutuu me balu and me uripen.

3.) Te katammanan, migtambahan din si Timutiyu ke egmenuwen egkeyimu meupiya suluhuanen of Hisu Kristu, and concerning the katungdanan din diye te me Kristuwanen.
