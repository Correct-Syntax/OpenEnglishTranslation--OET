Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text in advance before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 16th century English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

Galatians

Introduction

Te timpu imbunsuran e te pegwali ka Meupiya Panugtulen concerning ki Jesus, and te indawat e te non-Jews, duen e migginguma inse. Iyan inse keilangan kun naa eg-ikul iya te Balaud of Muwisis ka person eyew egkeyimu sikandin malehet migpalintutuu. Mig-apul si Paul te kene keilangan sika su ka pegpalintutuu re ka malehet egpuunan te umul due ki Kristu, pinaahi rue egkeyimu eleg diye te tangkaan te God ka person. Piru diye te me punduk te believers te Galasya, ka sabeka prubinsya te Ruma diye te Asya Minur, due people migsupak ki Paul and migpalintutuu ka person keilangan egtuman iya te Balaud of Muwisis eyew egkeyimu eleg diye te tangkaan te God.

This Sulat of Paul te Matig-Galasya, insulat din eyew egpakalibed diye te malehet pegpalintutuu seeye se people neuyan te kene malehet teaching. Imbunsuran din this pinaahi te pegpangguhud din ke nekey ka egpuunan neyimu sikandin apustulis. Migpamalehet sikandin kene diye egpuun te geem te people ka pegkaapustulis din su diye iya te God and labew te langun, diye sikandin ipeuyan te God te non-Jews. Ne dutu, migpakannekal sikandin ka egpakig-apul ware lein paahi egkeyimu eleg diye te tangkaan te God ka people, gawas te pegpalintutuu re iya.

Diye te katammanan me kapitulu, intabak din seeye se migkahi te mareet kun ke kene egtuman te Balaud ka sabeka person. Migkahi si Paul te makaaldek-haldek ke eg-engked sikandan te keupiya and keyid‑u te God egpeuripen te Balaud of Muwisis. Migtambahan din again sikandan te keilangan egpasakup sikandan te Panisingan te God eyew egkabalbalawan sikandan and kenad egpekeikul te igkeupii te lawa ran makasesale.
