Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

Hebrews

Introduction

The seini sulat, ware nanengnengi ke hentew ka migsulat kayi, piru igpeendiye seini te sabeka punduk te me Hudiyu migmalintutuu egngaranan degma te Hebrews. Insulat seini su eyew egkalig‑en ka pegpalintutuu ran tenged su nana-nasian e ka keyirapan eg-antusen dan and in-el-elehan ka pegpalintutuu ran ki Kristu. Sikan naa, imbagget sikandan te talagsulat concerning the pegpalintutuu ran and impanulu din sikandan te si Hisu Kristu ka malehet and ka katammanan impakita te God te people kayi te ampew te tane.

Ne due seini se tatelu kamalehetan mig-ay-ayaran din te egpanulu: (1) Si Jesus ka ware bunsuranan and ware katamanan Anak te God. Ne labew pad sikandin te me prupita, me panalihan, and minsan ki Muwisis. (2) Si Jesus ka impeila te God ware katamanan talagpanubad. Labew sikandin te me talagpanubad diye te Tapey Kasabutan. (3) Ne pinaahi ki Jesus, ka migpalintutuu kandin egkaluwas te sale and te kamatayen.

Nahuhud diye te kapitulu 11 ka concerning the pegpalintutuu te mabantug me Hebrews puun pad dengan. Sikan naa, imbagget te talagsulat ka migmalintutuu eglig‑en te pegpalintutuu ran. Ne diye te kapitulu 12, imbagget din man‑e sikandan egpabulus ka egmatinumanen taman te katamanan, iyan de egtengtengan si Jesus. Igpaantus man‑e kandan ka me keyirapan and ka pegbayad-bayad egginguma kandan. Piru keilangan egkatahaan dan seeye se langun, pegdisiplina re kandan te God isip me anak din. Mig-impusan din ka seini sulat pinaahi te pegtambag din and pegpaney-paney rin.

Main components of this “book”

Introduction: The Anak iling ded te God 1:1-3

Labew si Kristu te me panalihan 1:4-2:18

Labew si Kristu engki Muwisis and ki Huswi 3:1-4:13

Si Kristu ka labew te langun talagpanubad 4:14-7:28

Labew ka kasabutan of Kristu du te langun me kasabutan 8:1-9:28

Labew te langun ka pegpanubad ki Kristu 10:1-39

The allegory te pegpalintutuu eg-ikulen ta 11:1-12:29

The katammanan pegpaney-paney and ka pegpanulu 13:1-25
