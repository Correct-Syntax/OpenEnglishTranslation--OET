Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text in advance before using in public.

Note: This Readers' Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn't necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 500-year old English. (It's designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what's actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you're wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that's a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.)

Note that greyed words are words that the translators consider were most probably in the mind of the writer, but as none of us can double-check with the original speakers or writers, the reader is free to disagree. They are clearly marked because we have tried to be as honest / transparent as possible.

1 Thessalonians

Introduction

The Tisalunika the iney siyudad of prubinsya of Masidunya sakup of nasud of Ruma. Migpanulu si Paul of lalag of God there to Tisalunika of peg-awe din e there to Pilipus (Me Himu 17:1-4). Te ware naluhey, due me pegsupak from Jews neenenduenan of preaching of Paul there to non-Jews (Me Himu 17:5-9). Ne dutu, napehes si Paul eg-awe there to Tisalunika and miggendiye sikandin of Birya (Me Himu 17:10-13). Te ware naluhey, miggendiyad e again sikandin of Atinas (Me Himu 17:14-15). Nataman, migpabulus e sikandin there to Kurintu (Me Himu 18:1-17). Te pegginguma rin there, nakarawat sikandin of story of Timutiyu duma rin of work. Impangguhuran sikandin of Timutiyu concerning the egkeulaula of group of believers there to Tisalunika.

This the First letter of Paul of Matig-Tisalunika, wrote din in order to pegbagget and of peglig‑en of believers there. Migpasalamat sikandin of story concerning the faith ran and of love ran. Impasuman-suman din also sikandan concerning the living din impapitew rin kandan of there pad sikandin kandan. Intabak din again the me inse from there to believers concerning the return of the messiah, and ke egpakaangken naan ded of umul ware egtamanan the nammatey e believers of not pad eglibed the Messiah. Inggamit of Paul the this letter in order to pegpangguhud kandan of it’s necessary continue sikandan eggimu of aldew-aldew work ran taheed of egtetahad sikandan of return of the messiah.

Main components of Paul’s letter

Introduction 1:1

The thanking and praising 1:2-2:16

The good story of Timutiyu 2:17-3:13

The living egpakapahale of God 4:1-12

The teaching concerning the return of the messiah 4:13-5:11

The ending warning 5:12-22

The ending 5:23-28
