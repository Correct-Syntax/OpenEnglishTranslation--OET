Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

1 Thessalonians

Introduction

The Tisalunika ka iney siyudad te prubinsya te Masidunya sakup te nasud te Ruma. Migpanulu si Paul te lalag te God diye te Tisalunika te peg-awe din e diye te Pilipus (Me Himu 17:1-4). Te ware naluhey, due me pegsupak egpuun te me Hudiyu neenenduenan te pegwali of Paul diye te kene me Hudiyu (Me Himu 17:5-9). Ne dutu, napehes si Paul eg-awe diye te Tisalunika and miggendiye sikandin te Birya (Me Himu 17:10-13). Te ware naluhey, miggendiyad e man‑e sikandin te Atinas (Me Himu 17:14-15). Nataman, migpabulus e sikandin diye te Kurintu (Me Himu 18:1-17). Te pegginguma rin diye, nakarawat sikandin te story of Timutiyu duma rin te himu. Impangguhuran sikandin of Timutiyu concerning the egkeulaula te punduk te migmalintutuu diye te Tisalunika.

The seini se An-anayan sulat of Paul te Matig-Tisalunika, insulat din eyew te pegbagget and te peglig‑en te migmalintutuu diye. Migpasalamat sikandin te story concerning the pegpalintutuu ran and te geyinawa ran. Impasuman-suman din degma sikandan concerning the peg-ugpe din impapitew rin kandan te diye pad sikandin kandan. Intabak din man‑e ka me inse egpuun diye te migmalintutuu concerning the peglibed of Kristu, and ke egpakaangken naan ded te umul ware egtamanan ka nammatey e migmalintutuu te kene pad eglibed si Kristu. Inggamit of Paul ka seini sulat eyew te pegpangguhud kandan te keilangan egpabulus sikandan eggimu te aldew-aldew himu ran taheed te egtetahad sikandan te peglibed of Kristu.

Main components of this “book”

Introduction 1:1

The pegpasalamat and pegdayan 1:2-2:16

The meupiya story of Timutiyu 2:17-3:13

The peg-ugpe egpakapahale te God 4:1-12

The pegpanulu concerning the peglibed of Kristu 4:13-5:11

The katammanan pegpaney-paney 5:12-22

The katammanan 5:23-28

It is our prayer that this Readers’ Version of the Open English Translation of the Bible will give you clear understanding of the messages written by the inspired Biblical writers.
