Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text in advance before using in public.

Note: This Readers' Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn't necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 500-year old English. (It's designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what's actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you're wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that's a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.)

Note that greyed words are words that the translators consider were most probably in the mind of the writer, but as none of us can double-check with the original speakers or writers, the reader is free to disagree. They are clearly marked because we have tried to be as honest / transparent as possible.

Romans

Introduction

The Sulat of Paul of Matig-Ruma impeendiye din of Jews and of non-Jews egpalintutuu and egsalig ki Jesus the messiah. Insulatan din sikandan so that egpakaandam of pegbebentaan din pegpanumbaley there to kandan. Migplanu si Apustulis Paul egtrabahu duma of Christians there to not just egkaluhey, egkataman eggendiyad sikandin of Ispanya pinaahi of bulig dan. Migsulat sikandin in order to pegpangguhud concerning the pegsabut din of faith of Kristuwanen and of aldew-aldew living isip Christians. This document, nasulatan of kumplitu explaining of Paul concerning the story din.

Impangemusta rin the believers there to Ruma, and impangguhuran din of in-ampuan din sikandan. Pegkapenga rue, ingguhud e of Paul the theme of letter din, “Su pinaahi of good message, impanengneng of God ke immenu rin of eg-isip righteous/honest/sensible the people.” (1:17a). The langun person, Hudiyu ma and the not, it’s necessary egkeyimu righteous/honest/sensible there to tangkaan of God pinaahi of faith ki Jesus the messiah.

The seup impangguhud of Paul iyan the new umul pinaahi of unity ki the messiah there egpangkus of new rilasyun there to God. Duen e keupianan of believers duma of God and inluwas sikandan pinaahi of geem of Spirit of God puun of geem of sale and of kamatayen. Te kapitulu 5-8, migpangguhud si Paul concerning the tuyu of Law of God and of geem of Spirit of God due of believers.

Migpakannekal si Paul egkatabak din the inse ke egmenuwen egpakatupu the Jews and the non-Jews of planu of God for of langun person. Impasabut of Paul part e of planu of God the peggubabe of Jews of not just egkaluhey there egpangkus of igpakawali the good message there to non-Jews. But concerning the peg-alam of God of Jews, indakelan din ded sikandan of love tenged of saad din of kaap-apuan dan. Puun here, the langun person egpakarawat of love and mercy of God pinaahi ki the messiah Jesus.

Te ending, migpanengneng si Paul ke egmenuwen of living isip Kristuwanen, labew en iya of paahi of pegparawdawatey and pegpaheyheyinaweey of tagse sabeka. Inlagkes din also the me theme iling of pegpamakey of God, the katungdanan it’s necessary eggimuwen of Christians there to gubirnu and of tagse sabeka, and the me inse concerning the kunsinsya. In-epus of Paul the letter din pinaahi of greetings rin and of me duma rin, and of pegsaye din of God.

Main components of this “book”

Introduction and theme 1:1-17

Migkeilangan the person of kaluwasan 1:18-3:20

The paahi of God of pegluwas of person 3:21-4:25

The new umul due of ki the messiah 5:1-8:39

The planu of God concerning the Israel 9:1-11:36

The living of Kristuwanen 12:1-15:13

The ending and me pangemusta 15:14-16:27
