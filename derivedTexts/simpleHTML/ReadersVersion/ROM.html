Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

Romans

Introduction

The Sulat of Paul te Matig-Ruma impeendiye din te me Hudiyu and te kene me Hudiyu egpalintutuu and egsalig ki Hisu Kristu. Insulatan din sikandan eyew egpakaandam te pegbebentaan din pegpanumbaley diye te kandan. Migplanu si Apustulis Paul egtrabahu duma te me Kristuwanen diye te kene de egkaluhey, egkataman eggendiyad sikandin te Ispanya pinaahi te bulig dan. Migsulat sikandin eyew te pegpangguhud concerning the pegsabut din te pegpalintutuu te Kristuwanen and te aldew-aldew peg-ugpe isip me Kristuwanen. The seini baseen, nasulatan te kumplitu pegpasabut of Paul concerning the story din.

Impangemusta rin ka migmalintutuu diye te Ruma, and impangguhuran din te in-ampuan din sikandan. Pegkapenga rue, ingguhud e of Paul ka uluwan te sulat din, “Su pinaahi te Meupiya Panugtulen, impanengneng te God ke immenu rin te eg-isip matareng ka people.” (1:17a). The langun person, Hudiyu ma and se kene, keilangan egkeyimu matareng diye te tangkaan te God pinaahi te pegpalintutuu ki Hisu Kristu.

The seup impangguhud of Paul iyan ka iyam umul pinaahi te pegsabeka ki Kristu diye egpangkus te iyam rilasyun diye te God. Duen e keupianan te migmalintutuu duma te God and inluwas sikandan pinaahi te geem te Panisingan te God puun te geem te sale and te kamatayen. Te kapitulu 5-8, migpangguhud si Paul concerning the tuyu te Balaud te God and te geem te Panisingan te God due te migmalintutuu.

Migpakannekal si Paul egkatabak din ka inse ke egmenuwen egpakatupu ka me Hudiyu and ka kene me Hudiyu te planu te God para te langun person. Impasabut of Paul baad e te planu te God ka peggubabe te me Hudiyu te kene de egkaluhey diye egpangkus te igpakawali ka Meupiya Panugtulen diye te kene me Hudiyu. Piru concerning the peg-alam te God te me Hudiyu, indakelan din ded sikandan te geyinawa tenged te saad din te kaap-apuan dan. Puun kayi, ka langun person egpakarawat te geyinawa and hid‑u te God pinaahi ki Kristu Jesus.

Te katammanan, migpanengneng si Paul ke egmenuwen te peg-ugpe isip Kristuwanen, labew en iya te paahi te pegparawdawatey and pegpaheyheyinaweey te tagse sabeka. Inlagkes din degma ka me uluwan iling te pegpamakey te God, ka katungdanan keilangan eggimuwen te me Kristuwanen diye te gubirnu and te tagse sabeka, and ka me inse concerning the kunsinsya. In-epus of Paul ka sulat din pinaahi te pegpangemusta rin and te me duma rin, and te pegsaye din te God.

Main components of this “book”

Introduction and uluwan 1:1-17

Migkeilangan ka person te kaluwasan 1:18-3:20

The paahi te God te pegluwas te person 3:21-4:25

The iyam umul due te ki Kristu 5:1-8:39

The planu te God concerning the Israel 9:1-11:36

The peg-ugpe te Kristuwanen 12:1-15:13

The katammanan and me pangemusta 15:14-16:27
