Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text in advance before using in public.

Note: This Readers' Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn't necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 500-year old English. (It's designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what's actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you're wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that's a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.)

Note that greyed words are words that the translators consider were most probably in the mind of the writer, but as none of us can double-check with the original speakers or writers, the reader is free to disagree. They are clearly marked because we have tried to be as honest / transparent as possible.

2 Kings

Introduction

The Igkarangeb King sumpul of story concerning the daruwa kingdom of me Israilita ware neimpusi there to Hun‑a King. This Libru nabaad of daruwa: 1.) The story concerning the daruwa kingdom puun of egliwaraanan the igkasiyam siglu of not yet born si Jesus until natalu e the Samarya, and nataman this of neimpus e the kingdom there to igkahibang balabahan of leg‑un 722 of not yet born the Messiah. 2.) The story concerning the kingdom of Huda puun of pegkatalu of kingdom of Israel until pegkalundus and of pegkahuhus of Jerusalem pinaahi ki King Nibukadnisar King of Babilunya of leg‑un 586 of not yet born the Messiah. Diye neimpusi the story of neyimu e gubirnadur si Gidalya there to Huda diralem of pegmandu of Babilunya, and of impaligwe e si King Hihuwakin naprisu there to Babilunya.

This me karereetan, neyitabu there to nasud of Israel puun su ware migtuman the kings and the me people of Huda of God. The pegkahuhus of Jerusalem and the pegkasakup of me people of Huda iyan in-isip pinakarakel neyitabu of story of Israel.

Si Prupita Ilisyu the very neila here of Igkarangeb King. Si Ilisyu the migsubal ki prupita Ilyas.

Main components of this “book”

The nabaad peghari (1:1–17:41)

a. The prupita si Ilisyu (1:1–8:15)

b. The kings of Huda and of Israel (8:16–17:4)

c. The pegkatalu of Samarya (17:5-41)

The peghari of Huda (18:1–25:30)

a. Ligkat ki Isikiyas peendiye of ki Husiyas (18:1–21:26)

b. The peghari of Husiyas (22:1–23:30)

c. The final King of Huda (23:31–24:20)

d. The pegkatalu of Jerusalem (25:1-30)
