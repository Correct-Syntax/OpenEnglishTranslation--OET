ESFM v0.5 REV

Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text in advance before using in public.

Note: This Readers' Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn't necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 500-year old English. (It's designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what's actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you're wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that's a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.)

Note that greyed words are words that the translators consider were most probably in the mind of the writer, but as none of us can double-check with the original speakers or writers, the reader is free to disagree. They are clearly marked because we have tried to be as honest / transparent as possible.

Revelation

Introduction

The Impakita of Jesus the messiah ki John concerning the egkeyitabu keureme. Nasulat this dengan of timpu imbaybayaran the believers tenged of faith ran of Master si Jesus the messiah. Iyan dakel tuyu of John of pegsulat here the pegbehey of egkapallateng there to believers and pegbagget kandan so that egpakapabulus egpalintutuu minsan egbaybayaran sikandan.

Iyan kinarakelan part of it’s written here document the impakita of Jesus the messiah ki John the me palinneu very kein-inuwan. Ne that langun palinneu, due iya kaluwasan and igpasabut. Te that timpu, nasabut buwa of Christians but kuntee, due duma me palinneu very malised egkasabut, and various the me suman-suman of people of pegluwas here. But minsan pad iling due, due also me palinneu malemu just egkasabut iling of sungey iyan igpasabut the geem and katenged, and the kampilan iyan igpasabut the samuk. Inlibed-libed of kamasulug and of various paahi the me theme of this document pinaahi of me palinneu. But malemu just egkasabut the very important it’s written here: egdereetan iya of God the langun enemy din pinaahi ki the messiah Jesus, and egdasahan din also the people din migpabulus migpamakey, and egpakalemung sikandan kandin of ware egtamanan. Ne due egpakapahale kanta there to ending kapitulu, this concerning the new langit and tane eggimuwen of God and concerning the new Jerusalem egtuntunen puun of langit.

Main components of this “book”

Introduction 1:1-8

The first impakita ki John the hendue of dream 1:9-20

The me letter igpeendiye of seven group of believers 2:1-3:22

The linulun document due seven piket 4:1-8:1

The seven trumpita 8:2-11:19

The salimbaweng and the daruwa makaliyas-liyas mananap 12:1-13:18

The various impakita ki John the hendue of dream 14:1-15:8

The seven yahung langet of God 16:1-21

The pegkahuhus of Babilunya, and the pegkatalu of langun enemy of God 17:1-20:10

The ending judging 20:11-15

The new langit and tane, and the new Jerusalem 21:1-22:5

The ending 22:6-21
