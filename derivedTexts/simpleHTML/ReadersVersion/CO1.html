Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text in advance before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or 16th century English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

1 Corinthians

Introduction

The first sulat of Paul te Matig-Kurintu, insulat din te timpu diye sikandin te Ipisu ka egwali. Insulat din this in order to helping te me prublima nabayaan te nalein-lein punduk te believers impes-ek din diye te Kurintu. The Kurintu ka iney siyudad te prubinsya te Akaya, ka Akaya, sabeka te prubinsya sakup te Ruma. Te seeye timpu, sabeka dakel siyudad ka Kurintu nangugpaan te me Grigu, Rumanhen, and Jews. Ne puun su ilis sika te dahat, masalig also ka egmangendiye eg-untud te me barku ka egpamelegye and egpameli. Nabantug ka sika siyudad tenged te karatu din, and masalig ka nalein-lein rilihiyun te mahinged dutu, and masalig also ka egmeyibhilabetey dutu and ka eg-ikul te tapey batasan iling due. Hapit migdaruwa leg‑un si Paul ka mig-ugpe diye in order to pegwali, and masalig ka migpalintutuu (Me Himu 18).

Peglihad te me tatelu leg‑un, taheed te diye si Paul te Ipisu ka egwali, nakarawat sikandin te sulat puun te believers diye te Kurintu (1 Kur. 7:1). Nakarineg again sikandin concerning the ehetanan te believers and te duma pad me prublima ran diye te punduk te believers. Te this sulat, intambahan din sikandan concerning due and egpepaaween din seeye se egmeyibhilabetey sakup te punduk dan te believers. Intabak din again seeye se me inse concerning the egmaas-asaweey, me balu, me dasag te Panisingan te God, and ka peg-uyag te me minatey.

Te kapitulu 13, nahuhud due sika se geyinawa, iyan sika ka subla meupiya dasag te God diye te keet-etawan din. Ne kema ke sika iya ka amana ipanengneng te this baseen.
