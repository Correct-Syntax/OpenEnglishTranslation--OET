Note: This is still a very early look into the unfinished text of the Open English Translation of the Bible. Please double-check the text before using in public.

Note: This Readers’ Version is a translation into contemporary English aimed at the person on the street who hasn’t necessarily been brought up with exposure to Biblical jargon and/or King James English. It’s designed to be used alongside the Literal Version which gives the English reader a window into what’s actually written in the original languages. (See the introduction for more details—we recommend that you read the introduction first if you’re wanting to read and understand the Literal Version.) Between the two versions you should be able to easily read the clear message of the text, while at the same time being able to check for yourself if that’s a fair translation of what the original authors had inked onto their ancient manuscripts.

Genesis

Introduction

The kaluwasan te lalag Hinisis, “bunsuranan”. Seini baseen, kayi nasulat ka peggimu te langit and te tane, ka migpuunan te person, and ka bunsuranan te sale and peg-antus kayi te world. Nasulat man‑e kayi ka story concerning the keet-etawan in-alam te God and ka peg-uyan din kandan diye te inged insaad din kandan.

Nabaad te daruwa seini se baseen:

1.) The peggimu te world and ka story concerning the people. Kayi egkabasa ka story concerning ki Adan and ki Iba, ka concerning ki Kain and ki Abil, and ka concerning ki Noah and ka pegsipuk te weyig, and ka concerning the matikang house diye te Babil.

2.) The story concerning the first elders te Israilita. Si Abraham ka first elder te Israilita neyimu mabantug tenged te pegpalintutuu and pegsalig din and te pegtuman din te suhu te God. Egpakasinundul degma ka story concerning the anak din si Isaac, and ka apu din si Jacob (egngaranan ded degma ki Israel), and ka sapulu and daruwa (12) anak of Jacob iyan migpuunan te sapulu and daruwa (12) descendants te Israel. Nahuhud degma kayi ka concerning the anak of Jacob si Husi and ka neyitabu kandin iyan impuunan te nekeendiye te Egypt si Jacob duma te me anak din.

Egkabasa ta man‑e kayi ka story concerning the nalein-lein people nabuhal-buhal kayi te ampew te tane and migmannenasud e. Labew te langun, nahuhud kayi ka dakel himu te God peggimu te langit and tane and te langun nakatahu kayi.

Diye te katamanan te story, egkahuhud ka ware egtamanan saad te God te keet-etawan din. Kene eglingawan te God ka saad din and kene din iya egbalaharen ka keet-etawan din su egpanuluen and egbulihan din sikandan. Ne sikandin ka eggukum and eglegpad te langun eggimu te mareet.
